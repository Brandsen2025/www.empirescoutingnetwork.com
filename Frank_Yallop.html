Player Overview

Frank Yallop was a quintessential professional, a defender whose game was built on intelligence, positioning, and consistency rather than overwhelming physicality. At Ipswich Town, he was a cornerstone of the defense for over a decade, adapting from the old First Division to the Premier League. His strong reading of the game (POI) and clean tackling (TAC) made him a reliable performer. He later brought this experience to MLS, where he became one of the league's original standout defenders and a multiple-time All-Star, demonstrating his class and adaptability.
