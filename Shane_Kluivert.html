Player Overview

Shane Kluivert is a Dutch youth international and one of the most famous prospects in European football, thanks to his "generational" pedigree. He is the son of Patrick Kluivert and brother to Justin and Ruben. A product of two of the world's best academies, he moved from PSG's youth setup to Barcelona's "La Masia" in 2017. He is a skillful, right-footed left-winger known for his flair, pace, and 1v1 ability. He is currently a star for the Barcelona U19 team, competing in the UEFA Youth League and playing for the Netherlands U19 national team.
