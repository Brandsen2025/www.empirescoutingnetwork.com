Player Overview

Nicolás "Nico" González is a Spanish professional midfielder who is one of the world's premier young "pivots." The son of Deportivo legend Fran González, Nico was developed at Barcelona's La Masia. He is a modern defensive midfielder in the "Guardiola Positional" mold, combining a tall 1.88m frame with "Barça DNA"—elite vision, press resistance, and "Very Strong" passing. After a successful spell at FC Porto where he won the 2023-24 Taça de Portugal, he was signed by Manchester City for €60M in February 2025 to be their long-term #6.
