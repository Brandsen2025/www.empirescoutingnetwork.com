Player Overview

Rainer Bonhof was a technically gifted, tactically versatile midfielder and wing‑back noted for powerful long shots, set‑piece delivery and late forward surges; he featured centrally for Borussia Mönchengladbach and was a key member of West Germany’s 1974 World Cup winning side.
