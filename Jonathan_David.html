Player Overview

Jonathan David is an elite modern centre-forward defined by his intelligent movement (POI), clinical finishing (GOL, STK), and high-intensity work rate (TPI). He is not a traditional target man, but rather a mobile striker who excels at finding space between defenders (RDS) and timing his runs perfectly. He is strong with both feet, a reliable penalty taker, and contributes significantly to his team's defensive press from the front. His consistent goal output in a Top 5 league makes him one of the most sought-after strikers in Europe.
