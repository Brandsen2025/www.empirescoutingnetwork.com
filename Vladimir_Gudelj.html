Quick Summary

Brother of Nebojša and uncle to Nemanja/Dragiša. Vladimir was a classic prolific Yugoslav/Serbian striker who holds the record as one of Celta Vigo's all-time top goalscorers. His profile is defined by ruthless **Goal Threat (GOL)** and impeccable positional sense (**RDS**), showcasing extreme loyalty during his long career in Spain.

Player Overview

Vladimir Gudelj was the epitome of the classic goal poacher. His FGA score is high due to his consistent ability to convert chances (high **FIN**) and his capacity to find space inside the box. His career longevity at Celta Vigo, spanning parts of the late 80s and early 90s, established the Gudelj name as one of high quality and dedication in Spanish football.
