Player Overview

Rio Ngumoha is a "spectacular" and "explosive winger" regarded as one of the leaders of England's 2008 generation. His game is defined by "electric pace and acceleration" (PAC 10.0 P) and a "low centre of gravity" (1.70m) that gives him superb agility. He is an "extremely vertical player" who thrives in 1v1s with "quick footwork" and "high on-the-ball bravery". Unusually, he is genuinely two-footed, able to "shoot, pass, and dribble" with his weaker left foot. He has already broken records as Liverpool's youngest FA Cup player and youngest goalscorer.
