Player Overview

Franz "Der Kaiser" Beckenbauer is synonymous with tactical intelligence, technical elegance, and leadership (elite POI, ELE, CHR). He revolutionized defensive play by transforming the sweeper role into an attacking launchpad, stepping into midfield with grace and purpose. His effortless control (CPX), pinpoint passing range (LGP), anticipation (INT), and commanding presence made him the dominant figure for Bayern Munich and West Germany during their most successful periods.
