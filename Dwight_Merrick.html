Player Overview

Dwight Merrick is a Jamaican wide attacker who progressed through Mount Pleasant FA and Dunbeholden FC before signing for Montego Bay United FC in July 2025. Quick, direct and improving in finishing, Merrick is best deployed as an inverted winger or transition finisher in systems that prioritise pace into the penalty area.
