Player Overview

Ronaldo, known as "O Fenômeno", is one of the most complete strikers in football history, combining explosive acceleration, exceptional finishing, and elite dribbling to produce repeated match-defining performances at club and international level, including two World Cup titles and three FIFA World Player of the Year awards.
