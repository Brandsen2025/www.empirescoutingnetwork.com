Player Overview

Eusébio da Silva Ferreira, the "Black Panther," was a complete and explosive forward who defined an era. His game was built on a rare blend of cheetah-like acceleration (SPR 9.8), powerful athleticism (PHY), and a devastatingly hard and accurate shot with both feet (STK 9.7). He was a prolific goalscorer (GOL 0.95+) and a brilliant dribbler (DXT, FLA), capable of scoring from anywhere. His performance at the 1966 World Cup, where he won the Golden Boot, is one of the all-time great individual tournament displays.
