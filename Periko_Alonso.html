Player Overview

Miguel Ángel "Periko" Alonso is a Spanish football legend and the father of two La Liga midfielders, Mikel and Xabi. He was a dominant defensive midfielder, a "classic #6" defined by his relentless work rate, tough tackling, and tactical intelligence. He was the heart of the greatest team in Real Sociedad's history, leading them to consecutive La Liga titles in 1980-81 and 1981-82. His success earned him a move to FC Barcelona, where he won a third La Liga title in 1984-85. He was also a key member of the Spain squad for the 1982 World Cup.
