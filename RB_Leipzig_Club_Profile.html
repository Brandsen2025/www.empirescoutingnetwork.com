Club overview

RB Leipzig, founded in 2009, represents a modern football project defined by rapid ascent through German leagues, a high-intensity pressing and transition-based tactical identity, and a strong focus on developing young, athletic talent within the Red Bull global network. Their success includes multiple DFB-Pokal wins and consistent participation in the UEFA Champions League.
