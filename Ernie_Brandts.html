Player Overview

Ernstus "Ernie" Brandts is a robust, intelligent Dutch defender remembered for positional intelligence, aerial competence and rare big-game scoring moments. A product of De Graafschap, Brandts established himself at PSV where he won domestic titles and the 1977–78 UEFA Cup. Internationally he featured in the Netherlands' 1978 World Cup run, producing an extraordinary match vs Italy where he scored for both sides.
