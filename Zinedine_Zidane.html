Player Overview

Zinédine Zidane is regarded as one of football’s all-time elite playmakers, defined by sublime first touch, vision, passing range and the ability to produce match-defining moments on the biggest stages; World Cup 1998 and Euro 2000 winner, Ballon d'Or and multiple-time major trophy winner.
