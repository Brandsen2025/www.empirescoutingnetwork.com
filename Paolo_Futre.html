Player Overview

Paolo Futre was one of the most exciting wingers of his generation, a Portuguese dynamo defined by explosive acceleration (high SPR/DYN), exceptional dribbling (high DXT/FLA/CRK), and creative output (high KEY/SCA). Primarily operating as a left winger, he terrified defenders with his pace, close control, and ability to create chances from nothing. His low center of gravity, quick feet, and fearless approach made him a constant threat in the final third.
