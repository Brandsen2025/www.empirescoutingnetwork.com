Player Overview

Ruud Krol is a cornerstone figure of Total Football: a multi-positional defender who excelled as left-back and sweeper, renowned for two-footed passing, exceptional spatial awareness and the ability to instigate attacks from deep. A long-serving Ajax captain and Netherlands stalwart, Krol combined technical proficiency with leadership to anchor club and country across a golden era.
