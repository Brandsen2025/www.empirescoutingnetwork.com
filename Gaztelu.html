Player Overview

José Agustín "Gaztelu" Aranzábal is one of the most iconic and beloved players in the history of Real Sociedad. A true "one-club man," he was a tough, tenacious, and tireless central midfielder who represented the "heart" of the club for 15 seasons. He was a natural-born "caudillo" (leader), serving as the team's captain and mentoring the "golden generation" (Arconada, Zamora, Satrústegui) that would win La Liga shortly after his retirement. He was the ultimate "Ferguson Mental" player, a "warrior" whose "generational" leadership and loyalty (400+ appearances) made him a club legend. He is the father of fellow club legend, Agustín Aranzábal.
