He also scored the winning goal in the 2001 Intercontinental Cup.

Player Overview

Samuel Osei Kuffour is one of the most decorated African players in history. He joined Bayern Munich in 1993 and spent 11 seasons with the club, playing nearly 250 official matches. He was a physically dominant, "rocky" central defender known for his strength and elite tackling. Kuffour is famously remembered for pounding the turf in despair after the 1999 Champions League final loss. He achieved redemption two years later, winning the 2001 Champions League final and scoring the winning goal in the 2001 Intercontinental Cup, where he was named Man of the Match. He won 6 Bundesliga titles and 4 DFB-Pokals, and was named BBC African Footballer of the Year in 2001.
