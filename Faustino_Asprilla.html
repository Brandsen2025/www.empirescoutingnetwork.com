Player Overview

Faustino "Tino" Asprilla was one of the most electric, talented, and unpredictable forwards of his generation. His game was a whirlwind of explosive pace (high PAC), gangly yet unstoppable dribbling (high DRI), and pure, audacious flair (high FLA). He was a true "maverick" who could produce moments of impossible genius. He was a key member of Colombia's 1990s "golden generation" (57 caps, 20 goals), starring in the 5-0 win over Argentina and at two World Cups. At club level, he was an icon at Parma, winning the UEFA Cup Winners' Cup and two UEFA Cups, and is famously remembered at Newcastle for his hat-trick against Barcelona in the Champions League.
