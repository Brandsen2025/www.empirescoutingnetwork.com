Player Overview

Faustino "Tino" Asprilla is a Colombian forward celebrated for electric dribbling, sudden acceleration, unpredictable creativity and a high‑ceiling shot from range. His career peak at Parma and cultural impact at Newcastle United combine technical brilliance with a mercurial temperament that produced moments of match‑winning genius and intermittent availability issues.

Career Timeline

1988–1989: Cúcuta Deportivo (breakthrough). 1989–1992: Atlético Nacional (domestic star). 1992–1996: Parma (European success). 1996–1998: Newcastle United (iconic Premier League figure). 1999–2004: Series of returns across South America and Mexico; final retirement 2004.
