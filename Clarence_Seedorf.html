Player Overview

Clarence "Il Professore" Seedorf is one of the most successful and intelligent midfielders of all time. His legacy is unique: he is the only player in history to have won the UEFA Champions League with three different clubs. He first won it as a 19-year-old prodigy with Louis van Gaal's Ajax (1995). He won it again with Real Madrid (1998), and twice more during a legendary 10-year spell at AC Milan (2003, 2007). He was a complete box-to-box player, blending strength, stamina, "impeccable ball control," and a "master tactician's" brain. He was named UEFA's Best Midfielder in 2007.
