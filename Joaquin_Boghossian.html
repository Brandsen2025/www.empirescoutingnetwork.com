Quick Summary

Player Overview

Joaquín Boghossian was a formidable centre-forward, standing at 1.97m (6 ft 6 in). His game was built entirely around his elite size and strength, making him a dominant aerial threat (high AER) and a physical focal point (high PHY). His career peaked during a sensational 2009-2010 loan to Newell's Old Boys, where he scored 17 goals in 34 matches, becoming one of the top strikers in Argentina. This led to a €3M transfer to Red Bull Salzburg. He also won the 2011-12 Uruguayan title with Nacional.
