Quick Summary

A versatile, powerful forward who dominated three decades of German football. Started as a lightning-fast winger with a lethal left foot, evolved into a complete central striker, and finished as a midfield playmaker/leader. Euro 1980 Top Scorer and Cup Winners' Cup Champion.

Player Overview

Klaus Allofs was the total package. In his youth, he was all about **Sprint Performance (SPR)** and raw power, terrorizing defenses on the counter-attack for Fortuna Düsseldorf and Köln. As he aged, his **Mental Competence Score (MCS)** and **Vision (VIS)** took over, captaining Werder Bremen to European glory. He was a "Winner" archetype, lifting trophies at every club he played for.
