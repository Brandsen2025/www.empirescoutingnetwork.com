Jefferson Farfán is one of the greatest Peruvian players in history. An explosive, powerful winger who evolved into a clinical forward, he starred in Europe for over a decade with PSV and Schalke 04. Player Overview

Jefferson Agustín Farfán Guadalupe, known as "La Foquita," was a dynamic and powerful attacker who combined South American flair with European discipline. After dominating with Alianza Lima, he moved to PSV Eindhoven, winning 4 consecutive Eredivisie titles. He then became a legend at Schalke 04, known for his incredible speed and assists. For Peru, he is an immortal figure, scoring the decisive goal against New Zealand to send his country to the 2018 World Cup.
