Player Overview

Ivo Basay was a dominant centre-forward of his era, primarily defined by his outstanding aerial ability and clinical finishing inside the box (high GOL, RDS, STK). His physical strength (high PHY) allowed him to excel as a target man, holding up play and bringing teammates into the attack. While not known for blistering pace or intricate dribbling, his goal-scoring record across multiple top South American leagues highlights his effectiveness as a classic No. 9.
