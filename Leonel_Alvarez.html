Player Overview

Leonel de Jesús Álvarez Zuleta was the quintessential "Volante de Marca," a defensive midfielder who defined his era. He was the heart and lungs of Colombia's golden generation, providing the defensive foundation that allowed creative players like Valderrama to flourish. His game was built on relentless energy (high STA, high WRK), ferocious tackling (high TKL, high AGG), and superb tactical discipline (high IQ). He was a key player in Atlético Nacional's 1989 Copa Libertadores victory and earned 101 caps for Colombia.
