Player Overview

Carmelo Valencia was a veteran striker whose career was defined by longevity and a consistent ability to find the back of the net. A quick, right-footed forward, he was a "pressing forward" who excelled as a central striker. He had numerous prolific seasons, including 16 goals for Tianjin Teda in the Chinese Super League, and double-digit goal seasons for La Equidad and Cúcuta Deportivo in Colombia. He was also capped 3 times for the Colombian national team.
