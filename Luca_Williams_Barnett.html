Player Overview

Luca Williams-Barnett is one of the most highly-rated attacking prospects in English football. A two-footed playmaker, his game is defined by "masterful control and balance". He is a versatile threat, capable of dropping deep to start plays or finishing them with "spectacular" long-range goals. After dominating the U18 level (19 goals, 9 assists in '24-25), he was promoted to the U21s, where he won PL2 Player of the Month and is already prolific (6 goals, 4 assists in 7 PL2 games). He became Spurs' youngest-ever EFL Cup player at 16.
