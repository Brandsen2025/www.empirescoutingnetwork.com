Player Overview

Luis Suárez Miramontes was an elegant, cerebral midfielder renowned for vision, ball retention and range of passing; Ballon d'Or winner and a central figure in Barcelona and Inter Milan's tactical success in the 1950s–60s, plus a key component of Spain's 1964 European Championship triumph.

Key Matches & Milestones

  • Ballon d'Or winner 1960; first Spanish-born winner and formative individual recognition.
  • European Cup winner 1963–64 and 1964–65 with Inter Milan; pivotal deep-lying playmaker for Grande Inter.
  • UEFA European Championship 1964 — Spain champion and central creative contributor.
  • Represented Spain at 1962 and 1966 World Cups; sustained international influence across a 15-year span.
