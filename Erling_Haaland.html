Player Overview

Erling Braut Haaland is a force of nature, arguably the most effective pure No. 9 in world football. His game is built upon a foundation of extraordinary physical attributes – towering height, blistering pace (SPR), and immense strength (PHY) – combined with world-class off-ball movement (POI) and ruthless finishing ability (GOL, RDS, STK). He excels at finding space in the box and converting chances at an astonishing rate, consistently outperforming his already high xG.

Strengths and Weaknesses

Strengths

  • Finishing (GOL/RDS/STK): Generational ability to score goals; clinical with both feet and head.
  • Pace & Power (SPR/PHY): Unstoppable combination of speed and strength.
  • Off-Ball Movement (POI): Elite anticipation and runs to create space and receive passes.
  • Aerial Ability: Dominant in the air due to height and leap.
  • Mentality (CHR): Focused, determined, confident goalscorer.

Weaknesses

  • Link-up Play (Technical): Hold-up play and intricate passing still developing compared to finishing.
  • Dribbling (CRK/FLA): Relies more on pace/power than technical dribbling skill in tight spaces.
  • Right Foot Usage: Heavily left-footed, though capable with right.
  • Occasional Injury Concerns: Has had periods sidelined with muscular issues.
