Player Overview

Daniel Muñoz is a unique and elite attacking full-back. After becoming a prolific goal-scorer from defense in Colombia (7 goals in 27 games for Atl. Nacional) and Belgium (19 goals, 20 assists for Genk), he joined Crystal Palace in 2024. He has since become a revelation in the Premier League, operating as a hyper-aggressive wing-back. Statistically, he ranks as the #1 defender in the league for goal contributions, shots, and touches in the opposition box. His tireless engine (high STA/WRK) and intelligent "untracked runs" make him a constant threat. He was Man of the Match in Crystal Palace's 2025 FA Cup Final victory.
