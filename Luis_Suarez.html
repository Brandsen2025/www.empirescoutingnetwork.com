Player Overview

Luís Suárez is an all-time great centre-forward, renowned for his world-class goalscoring instincts (elite RDS, STK, GOL) and tenacious spirit (CHR). His peak combined relentless pressing (TPI) with brilliant movement (POI) and creativity (KEY, FLA). Now in his late career, his physical output (SPR, DYN) has declined, but his finishing, intelligence, and link-up play remain at a high level, allowing him to continue influencing games and scoring goals in MLS.
