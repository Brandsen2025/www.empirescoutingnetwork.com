Player Overview

Marcus Thuram is a French international forward who has successfully emerged from the shadow of his legendary father, Lilian. He began as a pacy winger at Guingamp before a breakout spell at Borussia Mönchengladbach, where he became a prolific goalscorer. In 2023, he joined Inter Milan on a free transfer and reinvented himself as a world-class centre-forward. He was the creative engine of Inter's 2023-24 Serie A title win, forming the "Thu-La" partnership with Lautaro Martínez and leading the league in assists. A 2022 World Cup finalist, his game is a rare blend of "elite" pace, power, and a "selfless" work rate.
