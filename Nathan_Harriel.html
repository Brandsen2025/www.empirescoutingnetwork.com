Player Overview

Nathan Harriel is a robust, athletic right-back whose game is built on a strong defensive foundation. He is elite in the air for a fullback, consistently winning headers (high Aerials), and uses his strength (PHY) and tackling ability (TAC) effectively in 1v1 duels. While his offensive contribution and on-ball technical skill (DXT, KEY) are areas for development, his reliability, pace (SPR), and defensive solidity make him a key component of the Philadelphia Union system and a strong prospect for the US national team pool.
