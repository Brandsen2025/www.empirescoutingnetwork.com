Player Overview

Johan Mojica is a prototypical modern attacking full-back, defined by his explosive physical attributes. His game is built on a foundation of elite pace (high PAC) and stamina (high STA), allowing him to relentlessly patrol the entire left flank. At 1.85m, he also possesses significant physical strength (high PHY). He is a key attacking weapon, used to provide width and deliver crosses. He has been a fixture in La Liga for over a decade (200+ apps) and was a starter for Colombia at the 2018 FIFA World Cup. He remains a key player for both his club, RCD Mallorca, and the Colombia national team.
