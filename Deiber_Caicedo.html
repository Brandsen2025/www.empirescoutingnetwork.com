Quick Summary

Player Overview

Déiber Caicedo is a dynamic and "crafty" winger, prized for his blistering speed (high PAC) and 1-v-1 dribbling ability (high DRI). At 1.69m, he has a low center of gravity, making him highly agile (high AGI). A product of the famed Deportivo Cali academy, he made 78 appearances before securing a move to Vancouver Whitecaps in the MLS as a "U22 Initiative" player. He won three Canadian Championships with Vancouver before returning to Colombia on loan, helping Junior FC win the 2023 Clausura title. He made his move to Junior permanent in January 2025.
