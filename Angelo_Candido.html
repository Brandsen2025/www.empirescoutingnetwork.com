Player Overview

Angelo Candido is a rare prospect in Brazilian football: a full-back whose game is built on defensive completeness. While many Brazilian full-backs are known for attacking flair and defensive lapses, Angelo stands out for his "enviable physical strength," strong positioning, and speed. He is described by his São Paulo U-17 coach, Mário Ramalho, as a "complete player" with "a lot of personality." This intelligence and confidence was shown when he took and scored a free-kick he wasn't assigned to, a move Ramalho praised as smart improvisation, not irresponsibility. His high potential was confirmed by a €5m pre-arranged sale to Strasbourg.
