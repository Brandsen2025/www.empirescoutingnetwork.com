Player Overview

Cody Mathès Gakpo is a Dutch professional footballer known for his versatility as a left winger and forward. Standing at 193 cm, he combines physical presence with technical skill, pace, and an eye for goal. A PSV academy graduate, Gakpo made his breakthrough in the Eredivisie before transferring to Liverpool in 2023, where he continues to develop as a dynamic attacking threat.

Career Overview

Gakpo began his youth career at FC Eindhoven before joining PSV's academy in 2007. He progressed through the ranks, making his professional debut with Jong PSV in 2016. After establishing himself as a key player for PSV's first team, he won the Dutch Footballer of the Year award in 2022. In January 2023, he signed for Liverpool, quickly becoming an important part of their attacking lineup.

Strengths & Weaknesses

  • Strengths: Versatility, physicality, finishing with both feet, dribbling, vision, and ability to create chances.
  • Weaknesses: Defensive contribution, occasional inconsistency in decision-making, and adaptation to Premier League physicality.

Global Impact Analysis

Gakpo's rise from the Dutch leagues to the Premier League highlights his growing influence on European football. His ability to impact games both as a scorer and creator makes him a valuable asset for club and country, contributing to the Netherlands' attacking prowess on the international stage.

National Team Career

Gakpo has represented the Netherlands at various youth levels and made his senior debut in 2021. He has since become a regular contributor to the national team, known for his attacking versatility and goal-scoring ability.

Career Milestones & Highlights

  • 2022: Dutch Footballer of the Year.
  • 2023: Transfer to Liverpool for a record fee.
  • Key goals and assists in Eredivisie and Premier League.
