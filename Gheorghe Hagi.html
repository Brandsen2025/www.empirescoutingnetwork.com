Player Overview

Gheorghe Hagi, "Regele" (The King), was one of the most technically gifted and creative playmakers of his generation. His game was a volatile mix of genius and fury, built on a magical left foot that could execute any pass, free-kick, or audacious long-range shot (AX 9.9). He possessed generational vision (DMA 9.8) and creativity (KEY 10.0), allowing him to dictate the entire flow of a game. He was the undisputed talisman of the legendary 1994 Romania World Cup team and a god-like figure at Galatasaray, leading them to a UEFA Cup.
