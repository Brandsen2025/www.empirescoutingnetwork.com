Player Overview

Gianluigi "Gigi" Buffon is widely regarded as one of the greatest goalkeepers of all time, defining his era through a combination of technical perfection, unmatched leadership (CHR 10.0), and incredible longevity. His game was built on flawless positioning (POS 10.0), which made difficult saves look routine. He was a master of composure (CMP 9.9) and a big-game specialist, famed for producing iconic, match-winning saves (CRS 10.0) when it mattered most. He was the on-field general for both Juventus and the 2006 World Cup-winning Italy team.
