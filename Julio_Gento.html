Quick Summary

Julio Gento was a Spanish professional footballer and a member of the legendary Gento family, brother of Paco and Antonio. Like his famous siblings, he was a pacy forward who played for 13 seasons in La Liga, most notably with Racing Santander and CD Málaga. He was a solid, reliable top-flight attacker who carved out his own long career.

Player Overview

Julio Gento López was the middle of the three Gento brothers, all of whom were professional forwards. While his brother Paco became arguably Real Madrid's greatest player, Julio built a very respectable career in his own right. He was a fast and skillful forward who played over 250 matches in La Liga, scoring 48 goals. He was a key player for CD Málaga and his hometown club, Racing Santander, where he is remembered as a local hero.
