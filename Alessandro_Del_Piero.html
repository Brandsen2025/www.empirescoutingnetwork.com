Player Overview

Alessandro "Pinturicchio" Del Piero was the definitive Italian "Fantasista" of his generation. He was a "No. 10" with the goalscoring record of a "No. 9." His game was pure art, defined by generational technique (TEC 9.9), creativity (KEY 9.8)[cite: 128], and an iconic, lethal finesse shot from the left channel—the "Zona Del Piero" (FIN 10.0). He was an elite free-kick specialist and the inspirational captain (CHR 10.0) [cite: 117] of Juventus, sticking with the club in Serie B and cementing his legendary status.
