Player Overview

Juan Manuel Asensi is a Spanish football legend and one of the most important captains in FC Barcelona history. He was a complete box-to-box midfielder, defined by his "generational" goal-scoring ability (101 goals for Barça), his "tireless" work rate, and his "clutch" leadership. He starred for Elche before moving to Barcelona in 1970, where he became the heart of the team. He was a key partner for Johan Cruyff in the 1973-74 La Liga-winning side and, as captain, he scored in the final to lead Barça to the 1979 UEFA Cup Winners' Cup. He was a 1980 European Championship starter for Spain and played in the 1978 World Cup.
