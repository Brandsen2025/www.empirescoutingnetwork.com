Player Overview

Julio César "Romerito" Romero is a Paraguayan legend, widely regarded as one of the best players in his country's history and the only Paraguayan named to the FIFA 100 list. He was a brilliant and complete attacking midfielder (#10), blending creativity, vision, and prolific goalscoring. He began his career at Sportivo Luqueño and starred for the New York Cosmos alongside Beckenbauer. His peak came at Fluminense, where he was the undisputed star, scoring 90 goals and leading the club to its historic 1984 Brazilian Série A title. His efforts earned him the 1985 South American Footballer of the Year award. He also won the 1979 Copa América with Paraguay.
