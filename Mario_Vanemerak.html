Player Overview

Mario Vanemerak was the engine of a championship team. An Argentine midfielder with Belgian roots, he arrived at Millonarios in 1987 and immediately became the team's leader. He was a tireless box-to-box midfielder, a classic "#8" who ran, tackled, passed, and led with a fiery passion. He was the "corazón" (heart) of the Millonarios team that won consecutive Colombian titles in 1987 and 1988, ending a 9-year drought. His non-stop running, grit, and leadership made him one of the biggest idols in the Bogotá club's history. He also had a notable career at Vélez Sarsfield in Argentina.
