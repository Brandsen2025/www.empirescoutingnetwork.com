Player Overview

José Leandro Andrade was a seminal figure in early Uruguayan football, nicknamed "The Black Marvel." A technically superb right half with exceptional spatial intelligence and composure, Andrade combined rhythmic ball control with progressive passing and calm leadership on the field; central to Uruguay's 1924 & 1928 Olympic triumphs and 1930 World Cup victory.
