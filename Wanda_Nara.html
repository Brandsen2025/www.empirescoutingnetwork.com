Agent Overview

Wanda Nara transformed from a model and TV personality into one of the most formidable agents in European football. Taking over Mauro Icardi's representation, she secured him the captaincy and lucrative renewals at Inter Milan before orchestrating a high-profile exit to PSG. She later facilitated his move to Galatasaray, where he became the highest-paid player in Turkish history. Her negotiation style is public, aggressive, and leverages her massive social media following and TV appearances (e.g., *Tiki Taka*) to apply pressure on clubs.
