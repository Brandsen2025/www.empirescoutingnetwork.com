Player Overview

Hugo Guillamón is the archetype of the Spanish defensive anchor. His **Position Intelligence (POI)** allows him to cover spaces and intercept passing lanes effectively. With the ball, his passing range (**LGP**) dictates the tempo of the game, launching counter-attacks or resetting possession. His versatility as a Center Back and CDM provides immense tactical flexibility for the manager.
