Player Overview

Lucas Cañizares Conchello is a professional Spanish goalkeeper and the son of legendary keeper Santiago. A product of the famed Real Madrid academy, he spent over a decade developing at Valdebebas, rising through the ranks to become a key player for Real Madrid Castilla. Known for his sharp reflexes—a trait he shares with his father—and his modern ability to play with his feet, Lucas is seen as a high-ceiling prospect. In 2024, he left Real Madrid for Tondela to accelerate his development with first-team football in the Portuguese Primeira Liga.
