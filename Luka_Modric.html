Player Overview

Luka Modrić is a midfield maestro whose influence transcends statistics. His game is built on exceptional technical quality (ELE, DXT), unparalleled vision and passing range (KEY, LGP) – famously utilising the outside of his boot ('trivela') – and supreme intelligence (POI, DMA). Despite his small stature, he possesses remarkable press resistance (CPX) and controls the tempo of the biggest games with effortless calm. A tireless worker and inspirational leader (CHR), his longevity at the absolute pinnacle of football is extraordinary.
