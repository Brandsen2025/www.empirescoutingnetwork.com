Player Overview

Andrea Pirlo redefined the deep-lying playmaker (regista) role with unmatched vision, passing range and set-piece craft. Transitioned from attacking midfielder to maestro under Carlo Ancelotti at AC Milan, Pirlo combined panoramic scanning with precision execution to control tempo and unlock defensive lines for club and country.
