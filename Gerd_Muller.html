Player Overview

Gerd Müller, nicknamed "Der Bomber", is one of football’s greatest pure goal scorers, famed for extraordinary efficiency inside the six-yard box, lethal instinct for rebounds and positional timing that produced record-breaking scoring tallies for club and country.
