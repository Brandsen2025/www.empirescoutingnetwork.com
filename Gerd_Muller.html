Player Overview

Gerd "Der Bomber" Müller was the ultimate goalscorer, a player whose entire game was singularly focused on putting the ball in the net (GOL 10.0). He lacked traditional athletic attributes like pace (SPR) or height-based power, but possessed generational intelligence (POI 9.9), anticipation, balance, and an instinctive finishing ability (RDS 10.0, STK 9.6). His "Torriecher" (goal-smell) allowed him to be in the right place at the right time, scoring hundreds of goals for Bayern Munich and West Germany, including the winning goals in a World Cup final and a European Cup final.
