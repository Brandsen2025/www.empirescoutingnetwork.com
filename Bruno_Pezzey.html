Player Overview

Bruno Edmund Pezzey was one of Austria’s finest defensive exports of the 1970s and 80s: a tall, commanding sweeper who combined aerial power with elegant long passing and a genuine scoring threat from set-plays. Successful in Austria and the Bundesliga, Pezzey anchored Wacker Innsbruck, starred for Eintracht Frankfurt during their UEFA Cup run and later strengthened Werder Bremen before returning to Austria to close his career.
