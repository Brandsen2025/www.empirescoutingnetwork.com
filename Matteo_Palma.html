Player Overview

Matteo Palma is an elite defensive prospect, blending a dominant 1.94m frame with impressive technical skill and tactical awareness. A "complete, dominant, and elegant centre-back," he is comfortable in a back three or back four and can also play right-back. He is known for his "high-risk, high-reward" anticipatory style, often leaving his line to intercept. After developing at Hertha BSC and Udinese, he made his senior Serie A debut in 2024 and has become a regular in the Coppa Italia squad.
