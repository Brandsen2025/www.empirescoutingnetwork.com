Player Overview

Oleksandr Piscsur was a specialist, a "pure" Target Man in every sense of the word. Standing at a massive 1.97m (6' 6"), his game was built on one simple, devastating premise: aerial dominance. He was a "battering ram" who terrorized defenders, winning nearly every header and excelling at holding up the ball. While he traveled to many clubs, his peak came in two places: at Volyn Lutsk, where he was a 20-goal-a-season man in the second division, and at Uzbek giants Bunyodkor. In 2013, he led Bunyodkor to a league and cup double, finishing as the **Uzbek League Top Scorer** with 19 goals.
