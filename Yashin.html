Player Overview

Lev Yashin revolutionised goalkeeping through unmatched reflexes, commanding area dominance and proactive sweep-style goalkeeping. The only goalkeeper to win the Ballon d'Or, his influence extended beyond shot-stopping to organisation, psychological control of the defence and penalty suppression; widely regarded as the greatest goalkeeper in football history.

Career Timeline

Youth and early sport: Dynamo factory teams and ice hockey; Dynamo Moscow senior debut 1950; established as Dynamo and USSR No.1 through 1950s–1960s; Olympic gold 1956, European Champion 1960; World Cup appearances (1958, 1962, 1966); remained with Dynamo until retirement in 1971; later coach and football official.
