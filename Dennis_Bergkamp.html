Player Overview

Dennis Nicolaas Maria Bergkamp was a footballing artist, a "master of assists" who defined the Second Striker role. Schooled in "Total Football" at Ajax, his game was built on a foundation of supernatural first touch (TEC 10.0) and generational football intelligence (POI 10.0). He had a 360-degree awareness of the pitch, allowing him to create chances from nothing (KEY 9.8) and score goals of breathtaking audacity and technical purity (FIN 9.7). He was the creative engine (94 PL assists) and "stylish inventor" for Arsenal's most successful teams.
