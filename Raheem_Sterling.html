Player Overview

Raheem Sterling is an English international winger who, after a high-profile emergence at Liverpool, became a cornerstone of Manchester City's domestic dominance. Signed by City in 2015, he was transformed by Pep Guardiola from a talented dribbler into one of the world's most effective goalscoring forwards. His peak from 2017-2020 saw him score 31, 25, and 20 goals in all competitions, respectively. His signature is a combination of explosive acceleration, low-gravity dribbling, and a unique, ghosting movement into the box to score tap-ins. A 4-time Premier League winner, FWA Footballer of the Year, and PFA Young Player of the Year, he is now a senior figure at Chelsea.
