Player Overview

Sergio "Checho" Angulo was a classic and prolific Colombian centre-forward. Standing at 1.83m, he was a strong and powerful presence in the box (high PHY), known for his poacher's instincts (high MOV) and clinical finishing (high FIN). He began his career at Deportivo Cali but achieved legendary status at rivals América de Cali, where he was a key part of the historic "Pentacampeón" dynasty that won five consecutive league titles (1982-1986). He was also a Colombia international, representing his country at the Copa América.
