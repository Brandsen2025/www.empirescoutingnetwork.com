Player Overview

Morgan Gibbs-White is an English international footballer who has established himself as one of the Premier League's most "electric and direct" attacking midfielders. A graduate of the Wolves academy, he truly exploded during a loan spell at Sheffield United in 2021-22, scoring 12 goals and adding 10 assists. This earned him a club-record move to Nottingham Forest, where he immediately became the team's talisman and primary creator. His game is defined by "elite acceleration," powerful ball-carrying, and a "high-risk, high-reward" passing style. A "relentless presser", he is also a serial winner at international level, having won both the FIFA U17 World Cup (2017) and the UEFA U21 European Championship (2023).
