Player Overview

Alphonso Davies is a unique tactical weapon, possessing truly generational pace (SPR 10.0) which he weaponizes in both attack and defense. His profile is defined by his ability to carry the ball progressively over long distances (elite Prog. Carries) and beat players 1v1 (elite Take-Ons). Originally a winger, his conversion to left-back at Bayern Munich created one of the most potent overlapping threats in world football. While his final product (Crossing, KEY) and defensive positioning (POI) can be inconsistent, his athletic ability to recover and join the attack is unmatched.

Strengths and Weaknesses

Strengths

  • Pace & Acceleration (SPR): Generational speed; arguably fastest player in the world.
  • Ball Carrying (Prog. Carries): Elite ability to carry ball from deep into attack.
  • Dribbling (DXT/Take-Ons): Exceptional 1v1 attacker, difficult to dispossess at speed.
  • Athleticism (PHY/DYN): World-class physical attributes (stamina, power, speed).
  • Recovery Speed: Able to make up ground defensively with his pace.

Weaknesses

  • Defensive Positioning (POI): Prone to being caught too high up the pitch; relies on recovery pace.
  • Final Product (Crossing/KEY): Inconsistent delivery and decision-making in the final third.
  • Technical Finesse (Passing): Short passing and control in tight spaces can be loose.
  • Injury Proneness: Has had several layoffs with muscular and heart (myocarditis) issues.
