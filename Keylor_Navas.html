Player Overview

Keylor Navas Gamboa is one of the most decorated goalkeepers of his generation, defined by world-class reflexes (SAV) and agility (DYN). His reputation was built on incredible shot-stopping (DSW), particularly in 1-v-1 situations (ONE), and performing at his best in the biggest moments (CRS), as evidenced by his role in Real Madrid's historic UCL 3-peat. While not a modern "sweeper keeper" in the mold of Neuer, his conventional goalkeeping skills, leadership, and composure (CPX) place him among the elite keepers of the 2010s.
