Player Overview

Ferenc Puskás was one of the most intelligent and prolific forwards in football history. His game was defined by a left foot of unparalleled power and precision (STK 10.0), combined with a genius-level understanding of the game (POI). Despite a stocky build and lack of conventional pace (SPR), he was a clinical finisher (GOL, RDS) and a brilliant creator (KEY, LGP). He captained the "Magical Magyars" of Hungary and, after defecting, became a legend at Real Madrid, winning three European Cups and scoring 4 goals in one final.
