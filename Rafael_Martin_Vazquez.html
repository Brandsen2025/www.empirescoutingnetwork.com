Player Overview

Rafael Martín Vázquez was the creative genius of the "Quinta del Buitre." While Butragueño was the ghost and Míchel the crosser, Vázquez was the "Enganche." He was an elegant, two-footed midfielder who dictated the tempo of the game with his sublime vision and a passing range that could unlock any defense. He was also a major goal threat from distance. He was a key pillar in Real Madrid's 5-straight La Liga titles before a high-profile move to Torino. He returned to Madrid to win another league title, cementing his status as a club legend.
