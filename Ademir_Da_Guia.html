Player Overview

Ademir da Guia, "O Divino," was footballing poetry. The son of the legendary defender Domingos da Guia, Ademir was the opposite: a pure, cerebral attacking midfielder. For 16 years, he was the heart, soul, and brain of Palmeiras, leading their "A Academia" teams that were the only side to truly challenge Pelé's Santos. Ademir played at his own, unhurried pace. He was a master of the "pause," holding the ball to draw in defenders before releasing a perfectly weighted pass. He was not fast, but he was "quicker" than everyone, as his brain was always two steps ahead. He is the all-time appearance leader (902) and a 5-time Brazilian champion, cementing his status as the ultimate "one-club" God.
