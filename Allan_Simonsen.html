Player Overview

Allan Rodenkam Simonsen is one of Scandinavia's most decorated forwards — a diminutive, technically superb attacker known for clinical finishing, intelligent movement and decisive goals on Europe’s biggest stages. European Footballer of the Year in 1977, Simonsen combined pace, close control and two‑footed finishing to succeed at Borussia Mönchengladbach and Barcelona and left a lasting tactical template for compact, creative wide forwards.
