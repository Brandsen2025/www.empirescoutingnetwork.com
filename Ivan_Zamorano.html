Player Overview

Iván "Bam Bam" Zamorano is regarded as one of the greatest headers of the ball in football history. His game was built on incredible leaping ability, physicality (high PHY), determination (high CHR), and clinical finishing (high GOL/RDS/STK). Beyond his aerial dominance, Zamorano was a tireless worker (high TPI), pressing defenders relentlessly and leading by example. He was a complete centre-forward capable of scoring various types of goals and imposing himself on any defense.

Weaknesses
