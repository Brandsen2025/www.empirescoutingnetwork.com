Player Overview

Endrick is a Brazilian "wonderkid" widely compared to legends like Romário and Ronaldo Nazário. He is a complete forward who blends explosive pace (high PAC), a stocky, powerful physique (high PHY), and a devastatingly powerful left-footed shot (high FIN, high POW). After smashing youth records (165 goals in 169 games) and winning two league titles with Palmeiras, he joined Real Madrid in July 2024. He is already a star for Brazil, famously becoming the youngest player to score at Wembley in 2024.
