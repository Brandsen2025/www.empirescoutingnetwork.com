Player Overview

Jeisson Lucumí is a dynamic winger whose game is built on elite pace (high PAC) and 1-v-1 dribbling ability (high DRI). A product of the América de Cali academy, he became a star in the Colombian league, which earned him a high-profile move to Atlético Nacional. He is versatile, capable of playing on either wing, and has won two Colombian league championships (with Junior and América de Cali). His career has also seen him play in La Liga 2 for Elche, in Mexico for Querétaro and Tigres UANL, and most recently in Angola before returning to Colombia with Deportes Tolima.
