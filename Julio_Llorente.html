Quick Summary

Part of the Gento/Llorente dynasty, Julio Llorente was a reliable and professional defender. Player Overview

Julio Llorente Gento was a highly respected Spanish defender, known for his professionalism, defensive solidity, and consistency. Like his brother Paco, he was a Real Madrid youth graduate and nephew of Paco Gento. He was part of the Real Madrid squad that won two La Liga titles. However, he is best remembered for his long and successful spell at CD Tenerife, where he played nearly 300 games. He was a cornerstone of the historic team under Jorge Valdano that famously defeated Real Madrid on the final day of the season in two consecutive years (1992, 1993), handing the title to Barcelona.
