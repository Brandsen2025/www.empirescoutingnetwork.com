Player Overview

Frank Rijkaard was the unstoppable engine and tactical brain for two of the greatest club teams of all time: Sacchi's AC Milan and Van Gaal's Ajax. Part of the iconic Dutch trio at Milan (with Gullit and Van Basten), he was a complete footballer who could dominate as a ball-playing centre-back or a powerful box-to-box defensive midfielder. He won the 1988 Euros with the Netherlands, 3 European Cups/Champions Leagues (scoring the winner in the 1990 final), 5 Eredivisie titles, and 2 Serie A titles.
