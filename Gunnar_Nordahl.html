Player Overview

Gunnar Nordahl, "Il Cannoniere," was a "force of nature" and arguably the greatest "pure #9" in Serie A history. A "fireman" by trade, he played with the strength and power of a "battering ram." He was the "spearhead" of the legendary "Gre-No-Li" (Gren, Nordahl, Liedholm) trio at AC Milan. He wasn't an "artist"; he was an "executioner." He led Milan to two Scudetti, won the *Capocannoniere* (Serie A top scorer) a record 5 times, and remains AC Milan's all-time leading goalscorer (221 goals). Before Italy, he was a 1948 Olympic Gold medalist with Sweden, a career he had to abandon (like his teammates) to turn professional.
