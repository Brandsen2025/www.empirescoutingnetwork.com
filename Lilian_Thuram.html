Player Overview

Lilian Thuram is a French football icon and one of the most decorated and respected defenders in history. His career is defined by "generational" intelligence, power, and versatility. He was the rock of the France team that won the 1998 FIFA World Cup (where he scored his only two international goals in the semi-final) and Euro 2000. At club level, he was a superstar in Italy's Serie A, winning the UEFA Cup with Parma as part of a legendary defense, before winning two Scudetti with Juventus. He finished his career at Barcelona. He was a complete defender: fast enough to be a world-class right-back and smart/strong enough to be a world-class centre-back.
