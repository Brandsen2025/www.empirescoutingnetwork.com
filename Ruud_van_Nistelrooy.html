Player Overview

Ruud van Nistelrooy was the epitome of a clinical striker - a player whose entire game was built around putting the ball in the net. His movement between defenders was masterful, his positioning impeccable, and his finishing ruthless. A pure #9 who lived for goals, scoring 150+ for Manchester United and 64 for Real Madrid in just five seasons.
