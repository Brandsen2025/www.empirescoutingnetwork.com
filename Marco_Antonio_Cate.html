Player Overview

Marco Antonio Catê was a technically gifted Brazilian attacker, typical of the flair wingers produced in the 1990s (high FLA, CRK, DXT). Possessing good pace (SPR) and dribbling skills, he was part of highly successful teams, including Telê Santana's São Paulo and Universidad Católica. His ability to operate in wide areas and contribute to attacking moves, including spells in Serie A and winning titles in multiple countries, highlights his talent, though detailed metrics are limited.
