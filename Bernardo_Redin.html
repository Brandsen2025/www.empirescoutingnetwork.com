Quick Summary

A legendary Colombian attacking midfielder and a key member of the famed "golden generation." Redín was the creative partner to Carlos Valderrama, helping lead Colombia at the 1990 World Cup (scoring 2 goals). He was a 3-time Copa América participant, winning third place in 1987.

Player Overview

Bernardo Redín was a highly intelligent and technical attacking midfielder, renowned for his creative partnership with Carlos Valderrama in Colombia's "golden generation". A product of the Deportivo Cali academy, Redín was a classic #10 who excelled with his vision (high VIS), passing (high PAS), and clever off-ball movement (high MOV). He was a standout player at the 1990 FIFA World Cup, scoring two goals for Colombia. He also had a successful club career, most notably with Deportivo Cali and their rivals América de Cali, where he won a Colombian Championship.
