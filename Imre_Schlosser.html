Player Overview

Imre "Slózi" Schlosser was the first "superstar" goalscorer in the history of continental Europe. A "mythological" figure from the pre-war era, his statistics are "Bican-esque." He was a prolific, intelligent, and technically gifted forward who dominated two different "dynasty" teams, first with Ferencváros and then with their rivals MTK. He won a staggering **13 Hungarian League titles** and was the league's **top scorer 7 times**. He was also the first player in history to score 500 official goals. For Hungary, he was a "goal-machine," scoring 59 goals in 68 caps.
