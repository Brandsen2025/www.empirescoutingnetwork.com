Player Overview

Jean Tigana was the engine of France's legendary 1980s midfield, combining relentless work rate with exceptional technical quality. Operating as a box-to-box midfielder, he possessed incredible stamina, intelligent positioning, and precise distribution. His ability to win possession and immediately transition to attack made him the complete modern midfielder before the term existed. A key component of both Bordeaux's domestic dominance and France's international success.
