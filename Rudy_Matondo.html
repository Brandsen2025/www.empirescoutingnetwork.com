Player Overview

Rudy Nzingoula Matondo is a high-ceiling central midfielder from the AJ Auxerre academy. He is a "brûleur d'étapes" (one who skips steps), having been fast-tracked from the U17s to the U19s (including UEFA Youth League) and the senior Ligue 1 team, all by age 17. His game is defined by a strong physical base (1.80m), relentless work rate (STA 9.2 P), and strong defensive instincts (TKL 9.0 P). He is comfortable as both a ball-winning DM and a box-to-box CM.
