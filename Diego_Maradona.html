Player Overview

Diego Armando Maradona Franco transcended football. His profile is defined by god-tier technical ability (perfect DXT, FLA, CRK), combining unbelievable close control, balance, and dribbling skill with extraordinary vision (KEY) and finishing (GOL, STK). Operating primarily as a classic No. 10, he dictated play, created countless chances, and scored spectacular goals. Beyond skill, his fierce competitiveness, leadership (CHR), and ability to perform under immense pressure (CRS) – most notably in the 1986 World Cup – elevate him to legendary status.
