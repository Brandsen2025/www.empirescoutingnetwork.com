Player Overview

Rodrigo Bentancur is a world-class Uruguayan central midfielder who combines the "Garra Charrúa" of a "tireless ball winner" with the "silky" technique of an elite playmaker. He began his career at Boca Juniors, winning two league titles before a high-profile move to Juventus. In Italy, he won 3 Serie A titles and 4 domestic cups, establishing himself as a top European midfielder. He joined Tottenham Hotspur in 2022, where he became an instant fan favorite for his "relentless" pressing and all-around game. A key player for Uruguay (70+ caps), he has played in two World Cups and is the engine of their midfield.
