Player Overview

Arturo "El Rey" Vidal is a Chilean professional footballer defined by his warrior-like presence on the pitch. He is a complete box-to-box midfielder who combines relentless tackling and stamina with impressive technical skill, passing range, and a consistent goal threat. After developing at Colo-Colo and Bayer Leverkusen, he became a global superstar at Juventus, forming a legendary midfield and winning four consecutive Serie A titles. He continued his success at Bayern Munich and Barcelona, becoming one of the few players to win titles in Italy, Germany, and Spain. His legacy is cemented by his role as the engine of Chile's Golden Generation, leading them to historic victories in the 2015 and 2016 Copa América finals.
