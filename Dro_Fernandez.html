Player Overview

Pedro "Dro" Fernández Sarmiento is one of the most exciting prospects to emerge from La Masia, drawing comparisons to Iniesta and Thiago. His game is defined by supreme intelligence (POI 10.0 P) and a "rare mix of technique... and fluidity". His first touch is "almost always oriented," allowing him to evade pressure and play one step ahead. A versatile attacker, he is comfortable as an AM, left winger, or even a False 9, and possesses outstanding set-piece ability. He has already made his senior La Liga and Champions League debuts in 2025.
