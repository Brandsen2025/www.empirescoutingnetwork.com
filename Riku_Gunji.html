Player Overview

Riku Gunji is a high-energy centre-forward known for his relentless movement and poaching instincts (high POS). He emerged as a top high school talent in Japan, scoring 18 goals for Ichifuna HS. He joined Shimizu S-Pulse in 2024, winning the J2 League and earning promotion. He scored his first J1 League goal in his first J1 match. At 1.76m, he is not a target man, but a mobile striker who thrives on intelligent runs and clinical finishing. He is a former Japan U18 international.
