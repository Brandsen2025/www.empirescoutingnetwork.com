Player Overview

Francis "Frankie" Westfield is a young, developing homegrown right-back for the Philadelphia Union. Praised by coaching staff for his energy, "on-ball quality," and "real intention" on the field, he profiles as a modern, energetic fullback. After a successful stint with Philadelphia Union II in MLS Next Pro, he signed his senior contract in February 2025 and is being integrated into the first team, already making key contributions in high-pressure situations like playoff shootouts.
