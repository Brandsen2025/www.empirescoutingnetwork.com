Player Overview

Elías Ricardo Figueroa Brander is a legend of world football, often mentioned alongside Beckenbauer and Baresi as one of the greatest defenders ever. "Don Elías" was an elegant, technical libero who was famed for his "clean" tackling (never receiving a red card in his 20+ year career), his aerial dominance, and his ability to start attacks from the back. He achieved legendary status at Peñarol in Uruguay (winning two titles) and at Internacional in Brazil (winning two undefeated Série A titles). He is the only defender to be named South American Footballer of the Year three times (1974, 1975, 1976). He captained Chile in three FIFA World Cups (1966, 1974, 1982).
