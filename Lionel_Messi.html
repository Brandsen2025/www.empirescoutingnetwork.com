Player Overview

Lionel Andrés Messi represents the pinnacle of attacking football, combining otherworldly dribbling ability (perfect DXT) and close control with extraordinary vision (KEY), creativity (CRK, SCA), and relentless goalscoring (GOL, STK). His low centre of gravity allows for incredible agility and balance, making him virtually unstoppable 1v1. Capable of scoring any type of goal and providing assists at an elite rate, he dictates the final third like few others in history. His evolution from winger to false nine to deeper playmaker showcases his immense football IQ (POI, DMA).
