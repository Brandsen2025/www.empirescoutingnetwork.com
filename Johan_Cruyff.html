Player Overview

Hendrik Johannes "Johan" Cruyff was a footballing visionary, the on-field embodiment and architect of "Total Football." His game was defined by supreme intelligence (POI/DMA 10.0), seeing the game in time and space. He combined this with generational technical skill (DXT, FLA), revolutionary dribbling (the "Cruyff Turn"), and elite creativity (KEY, SCA). He was also a prolific goalscorer (GOL). More than just a player, he was an on-field coach and leader (CHR) who fundamentally changed the clubs he played for (Ajax, Barcelona) and the sport itself.
