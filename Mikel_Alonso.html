Player Overview

Mikel Alonso is a Spanish former professional midfielder and the older brother of Xabi Alonso. Like his father Periko and brother, he was a product of the Real Sociedad academy. He was an intelligent, composed, and technical central midfielder, a "classic #6" who played with his head up. He became a "fixed value" and captain for Real Sociedad, taking his brother's place in midfield after Xabi left for Liverpool. While his career path diverged, he was a respected professional, playing in La Liga for Sociedad and Tenerife and having a brief spell in the Premier League with Bolton Wanderers.
