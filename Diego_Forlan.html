Player Overview

Diego Forlán was a complete and highly intelligent modern forward. While he struggled at Manchester United, he exploded in La Liga, winning two European Golden Shoes. His game was defined by versatility, "very strong" long shots, and elite technique. He was comfortable shooting with both feet, taking free-kicks, and dropping deep to create. His peak came at the 2010 World Cup, where he scored 5 goals and was awarded the Golden Ball as the tournament's best player, leading Uruguay to the semi-finals.
