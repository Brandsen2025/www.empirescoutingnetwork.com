Player Overview

Max Dowman is a "special prodigy" and one of the most advanced talents to ever emerge from Arsenal's Hale End academy. He is a "skilful, energetic, and naturally gifted" attacking midfielder (AM/RW) who plays with remarkable maturity. His game is defined by generational creativity (KEY 9.8 P) and vision (DMA 9.7 P), with an ability to "cut defences out of the equation". Despite his age, he is aggressive, determined, and a "nuisance" for defenders, often "out-muscling" older players. He holds records as Arsenal's youngest-ever starter and youngest UEFA Youth League goalscorer.
