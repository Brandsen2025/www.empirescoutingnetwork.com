Selection Criteria & Methodology

Minimum Requirements

  • Must have played for Boca Juniors
  • Minimum 20 official matches OR significant impact
  • Verified through multiple reliable sources
  • Excludes youth players without senior appearances
  • Excludes loan players with minimal impact

Impact Factors (Weighted)

  • Titles won with Boca (30%)
  • Matches played & goals scored (25%)
  • Historical significance to club (20%)
  • Fan recognition & cult status (15%)
  • International recognition while at Boca (10%)

Era Classifications

  • Amateur Era (1905-1930): Foundation legends
  • Professional Era (1931-1960): Early professionals
  • Golden Era (1960-1990): Classic period
  • Modern Era (1990-Present): Contemporary stars

Verification Sources

  • Official Boca Juniors records
  • Argentine Football Association data
  • Wikipedia with citations
  • Goal.com & ESPN archives
  • Multiple corroborating sources required
