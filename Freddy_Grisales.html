Player Overview

Freddy "Totono" Grisales was a hard-tackling, powerful central midfielder and a key component of Colombia's 2001 Copa América championship team. He was a legend at Atlético Nacional, where he played over 240 games across multiple spells. "Totono" was a complete midfielder for his era: a tough, combative ball-winner who also possessed a fearsome long-range shot and was a renowned free-kick specialist. He was highly respected in Argentina, where he starred for Colón, San Lorenzo, and Independiente. He earned 41 caps for his country, scoring 6 goals.
