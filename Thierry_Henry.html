Player Overview

Thierry Daniel Henry was a complete offensive weapon, an artist disguised as an athlete. He possessed generational pace (PAC 10.0), which he combined with a balletic grace and lethal dribbling (DXT 9.7). Starting on the left, his signature move was cutting inside to curl a finessed shot (FIN 9.8) into the far corner. He was more than a goalscorer (Arsenal's all-time leader); he was an elite creator, famously providing 20 assists in the 2002-03 season (KEY 9.0). He was the leader of the "Invincibles" and one of the most dominant, elegant, and intelligent (POI 9.5) forwards ever.
