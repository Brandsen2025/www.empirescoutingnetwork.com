Player Overview

Vasilis Hatzipanagis is widely remembered as Greece's greatest footballing technician, a mercurial left‑side creator who combined mesmerising dribbling with incisive vision. He rose to prominence at Pakhtakor Tashkent before becoming a lifelong idol at Iraklis Thessaloniki, where his artistry packed stadiums and earned him enduring cult status.

Career Timeline

1972–1975: Pakhtakor Tashkent (Soviet leagues). 1975–1990: Iraklis Thessaloniki (Alpha Ethniki mainstay). 1980: short loan spell in Canada (Toronto Panhellenic). Farewell appearance in European competition: UEFA Cup 1990 vs Valencia.
