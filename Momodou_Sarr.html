Player Overview

Momodou Sarr is a 25-year-old Gambian forward currently playing for FC Inter Turku in the Finnish Veikkausliiga. A tall (1.88m) and powerful attacker, he offers a significant physical presence and aerial threat. While primarily a centre-forward, his athleticism allows him to operate on the wing as well. After developing at VPS and IF Gnistan, he moved to Inter Turku in early 2025 and has become a regular part of their attacking rotation, contributing 2 goals and 1 assist in his first 14 league appearances.
