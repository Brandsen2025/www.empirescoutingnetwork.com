Player Overview

Facinet Conté is one of the most exciting raw talents in European football. His "main quality is his speed"—he possesses "supersonic" pace (high SPR) that terrorizes defenses. He had a meteoric rise, moving from amateur football to SC Bastia and the Guinea national team for AFCON. In July 2025, he completed a high-profile move to Young Boys, taking his talents to a Champions League-level club.

Weaknesses
