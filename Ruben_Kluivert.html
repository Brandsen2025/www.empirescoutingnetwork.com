Player Overview

Ruben Kluivert is a Dutch professional footballer carrying on his family's defensive legacy in the modern era. The son of Patrick Kluivert and a product of the FC Utrecht academy, he is a tall (1.87m), right-footed centre-back. He developed his game in the Eerste Divisie with Jong Utrecht and FC Dordrecht. His breakout 2024-25 season with Casa Pia in the Portuguese Primeira Liga showcased his strong tackling and aerial ability, earning him a €3.8M move to Ligue 1 giants Lyon in July 2025. He has already become a key player, scoring his first goal for Lyon in a Europa League match.
