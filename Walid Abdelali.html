Player Overview

Walid Abdelali is a 32-year-old French-Algerian defensive midfielder with a vast range of professional experience. Trained at the Montreal Impact academy and a former France U17 international, his career has taken him to numerous countries, including Finland (MP Mikkelin, SalPa, Pallo-Iirot), Iceland (Grindavík), and France (Vierzon F18). In June 2025, he joined Bhutanese champions Paro FC to bolster their squad for continental competition. He is a versatile, two-footed midfielder (1.80m) known for his tackling, passing, and aerial game.
