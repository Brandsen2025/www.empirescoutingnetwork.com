Player Overview

Nicolò Barella is a world-class Italian midfielder, widely regarded as one of the most complete "box-to-box" players in the game. After breaking out as captain of his hometown club Cagliari, he joined Inter Milan and became the heart of their midfield, winning two Serie A titles and reaching a Champions League final. He is the engine of the Italian national team, a key player in their historic back-to-back European Championship wins (2020 & 2024). Barella's game is a perfect fusion of grit and quality: he has a relentless work rate, bites into tackles, and possesses the technical skill to dribble, pass, and score.
