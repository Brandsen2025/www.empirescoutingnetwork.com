Player Overview

Johan Bakayoko is a modern inverted winger who attacks the near post from the right channel, leveraging a left foot to finish early and a low centre of gravity to execute tight directional changes. His profile suits high-tempo transition systems and possession templates that allow him to receive in the half-space and either carry or combine with an inside forward.
