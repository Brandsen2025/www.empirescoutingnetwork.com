Player Overview

Carlos Estrada was the tactical brain and midfield organizer of one of Colombia's most successful club teams. Operating primarily as a central or defensive midfielder, his intelligence, positioning, and passing ability made him the perfect link between defense and attack. A player who prioritized team structure over individual glory, Estrada's contributions were often understated but crucial to Atlético Nacional's domestic and continental successes.
