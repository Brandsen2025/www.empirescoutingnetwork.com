Player Overview

Nílton Reis dos Santos, "A Enciclopédia," was a footballing revolutionary. He redefined the left-back role, proving a defender could be both a 'stopper' and a primary attacking weapon. His game was built on supreme intelligence (POI 10.0) and technical skill (DXT 9.5), allowing him to dispossess opponents flawlessly (TKL 9.8) before embarking on surging, elegant runs. His endless stamina (STA 9.8) let him control the entire left flank for 90 minutes. He was the complete player, a leader and a two-time World champion.
