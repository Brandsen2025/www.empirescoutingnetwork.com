Player Overview

Xavier "Xavi" Hernández Creus was the embodiment of a footballing philosophy. He was the "metronome" and on-field brain of two of the greatest teams in history (Guardiola's Barcelona and Spain's 2008-12 dynasty). His game was not physical, but purely mental (POI 10.0, DMA 10.0). He dictated the tempo, controlled possession ("the passing carousel"), and broke down defenses with his generational vision and passing (PAS 10.0). His ability to find space, receive under pressure ("La Pausa"), and execute the perfect pass (KEY 9.8) was unmatched.
