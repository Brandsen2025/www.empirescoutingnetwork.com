Player Overview

Robert Frederick Chelsea "Bobby" Moore OBE was the definitive defensive artist. He redefined centre-back play, replacing brute force with intellect and elegance. His game was built on generational intelligence (POI 10.0), anticipating play seconds ahead of opponents. His tackling (TKL 10.0) was legendarily clean and perfectly timed. Beyond his defensive duties, he was a deep-lying playmaker, breaking lines with precise passes (PAS 9.5). As a leader (CHR 10.0), he was the "captain's captain," an icon of composure who led England to their greatest triumph.
