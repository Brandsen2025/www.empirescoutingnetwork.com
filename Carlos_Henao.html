Player Overview

Carlos Henao is a well-traveled Colombian centre-back whose defensive career has been completely defined by his extraordinary attacking weapon: a missile of a left-footed free-kick. While a solid and physical (1.82m) defender (good TKL, good PHY), his legendary status comes from his ability to score "bombazos" from 30+ yards, making him one of the most dangerous set-piece takers in the world. He has been a key player for many Colombian clubs, including winning a league title with Millonarios and the Superliga with Santa Fe.
