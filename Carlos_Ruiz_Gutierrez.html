Player Overview

Carlos "El Pescadito" Ruiz was a prolific and intelligent centre-forward, whose legacy is defined by his incredible goalscoring records for Guatemala and in MLS. While not physically dominant, he possessed elite box movement (POI, RDS) and a clinical finishing ability with both feet and, surprisingly given his height (1.75m), his head. His tenacity and knack for scoring in big moments (high CRS) made him a legend for club and country, winning an MLS MVP and Golden Boot in his debut season.
