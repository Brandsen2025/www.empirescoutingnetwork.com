Player Overview

Leny Yoro is a "modern defender" who combines a tall 1.90m frame with exceptional defensive intelligence and composure. Hailed as one of the best talents of his generation, his game is defined by his outstanding ability to read danger, make crucial interceptions, and his "strong" tackling. He is very calm in possession, preferring to play simple, accurate passes rather than just clearing the ball under pressure. After a breakout 2023-24 season at Lille where he was statistically one of the best duel-winners in Europe, he earned a major transfer to Manchester United.

  • Composure & Passing (CPX/PAS): "Calm" under pressure, plays "simple and consistent passes" with high accuracy.
  • Duel Success: One of Europe's best in both ground and aerial duels (2023-24).
  • Maturity: Impressive composure and spirit for his age.

Weaknesses

  • Progressive Passing: Tends to play simple passes, needs to develop line-breaking ability.

  • Aerial Duels (Context): Some sources list as a weakness, others a strength (70% win rate).
