Quick Summary

Kolo Touré was a legendary Premier League defender and a core member of the Arsenal "Invincibles." Renowned for his tenacious tackling, incredible pace, and passionate leadership, he won the league with two different clubs (Arsenal & Man City). He was also a key part of the 2015 AFCON-winning Ivory Coast team and one of the most beloved personalities in football.

Player Overview

Kolo Touré was one of the most formidable and successful African defenders in Premier League history. After famously injuring Arsène Wenger in his trial, he was signed by Arsenal and converted from a midfielder into a world-class centre-back. He was the athletic heart of the "Invincibles" defense alongside Sol Campbell. He later joined Manchester City, winning a second Premier League title, before becoming a cult hero at Liverpool and Celtic. He was defined by his all-action, passionate defending, his recovery pace, and his infectious leadership.
