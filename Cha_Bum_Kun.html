Player Overview

Cha Bum-kun is a legendary striker known for explosive pace, thunderous shooting and clinical finishing; succeeded in Bundesliga across Eintracht Frankfurt and Bayer Leverkusen, becoming one of Asia's all-time greatest forwards and a proven big-game performer.
