Player Overview

Michel François Platini, "Le Roi," was the definitive "No. 10" of his era, a footballing aristocrat who combined supreme intelligence (POI 10.0) with elegant technical genius (TEC 10.0) and prolific goalscoring (GOL 9.8). His game was defined by vision (KEY 9.9), reading the play faster than anyone else. He was a generational free-kick specialist and a leader (CHR 9.8) who captained France's "Magic Square" and led Juventus to European glory. He is one of only three players to win the Ballon d'Or three consecutive times.
