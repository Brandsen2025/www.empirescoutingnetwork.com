Player Overview

Ronaldinho is one of football’s most joyous creators, combining exceptional dribbling, inventiveness and technique with the ability to produce unexpected solutions that unlock defences; Ballon d'Or winner and central figure in Barcelona’s mid-2000s revival and Brazil’s 2002 World Cup victory.
