Player Overview

Jacob Brøchner Ambæk is a Danish professional footballer viewed as one of Brøndby's most exciting attacking prospects. A 1.85m centre-forward, he demonstrated a prolific scoring ability in the academy, finishing as joint top scorer in the U19 league and scoring 31 goals in 22 U17 league appearances during the 2024-25 season. This form earned him a promotion to the senior squad for the 2025-26 season, where he was given the #38 shirt. He scored his first professional goal in a Danish Cup match in September 2025. He is a regular for the Danish youth national teams.
