Player Overview

Luis Gabriel Suazo Secchi is a Honduran professional footballer with a prestigious pedigree. He is the son of legendary striker David Suazo, and was born in Cagliari, Italy, during his father's playing career. He developed at Cagliari's academy before being signed by Juventus in 2022. A pacy 1.83m left-winger, Suazo made a high-profile move to the Braga academy in July 2025. His status as an elite global prospect was confirmed in October 2025, when *The Guardian* named him one of the 60 best players born in 2008. Despite being eligible for Italy, he chose to represent Honduras, scoring a hat-trick for the U17s in February 2025.
