      <section class="section" aria-labelledby="overview">
        <h2 id="overview" style="color:var(--accent)">Player Overview</h2>
        <p class="lead">Alfredo "Chango" Moreno was an Argentine striker celebrated for his lethal finishing and immense charisma. He was a product of the Boca Juniors academy, breaking into the first team and contributing to a golden era that won the Copa Libertadores in 2000 and 2003. While successful at Boca, he became a true icon in Mexico, playing for 9 different clubs. His peak came at Necaxa and San Luis, where he won the Apertura 2007 top scorer award with 18 goals. He finished his career as one of the most prolific scorers in Liga MX history, with 146 goals. He tragically passed away from cancer in 2021 at age 41.</p>
      </section>
