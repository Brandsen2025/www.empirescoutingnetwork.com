Player Overview

Mathys Saban is a technically gifted young French attacking midfielder/winger with a promising skill set built on creativity, dribbling ability (DRB), and intelligent off-ball movement (POI). Having developed through Torcy and Saint-Étienne's youth academies, he's now gaining valuable first-team experience in Luxembourg's National Division with Racing FC Union Luxembourg. His game revolves around creating opportunities for teammates through clever passing (VIS, CRE) and taking on defenders in 1v1 situations. While still developing consistency in goal output, he shows the technical foundation and football intelligence that could see him progress to higher levels.
