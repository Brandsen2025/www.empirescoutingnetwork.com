Player Overview

Ignacio "Nacho" Russo is an Argentine professional footballer who has built a solid career under the pressure of a legendary family name. A product of the Rosario Central academy, he is a centre-forward who differs from his defensive-midfielder father. Nacho is a "pressing" forward, valued for his relentless work rate off the ball, his intelligent runs to create space, and his composed finishing. After several successful loan spells in the Primera Nacional and Primera División with clubs like Instituto and Chacarita, he is currently on loan at Tigre, where he has become a consistent goal threat.
