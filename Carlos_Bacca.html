Player Overview

Carlos Bacca is a world-class centre-forward defined by his lethal finishing, intelligent movement, and big-game mentality. After becoming a 4-time top scorer in Colombia and Belgium, he exploded at Sevilla, leading them to two consecutive UEFA Europa League titles (2014, 2015), including a two-goal performance in the 2015 final. He later won a third Europa League with Villarreal (2021). A 52-cap Colombia international, he was a complete striker who blended pace, power, and deadly instinct in the box.
