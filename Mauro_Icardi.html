Player Overview

Mauro Icardi is an Argentine striker known for his clinical efficiency and dramatic career arc. A product of La Masia, he turned professional in Italy with Sampdoria before becoming a superstar at Inter Milan. At Inter, he scored 124 goals, became captain, and twice won the Serie A Golden Boot. Despite off-field controversies often involving his wife/agent Wanda Nara, his on-field production has rarely wavered. At Galatasaray, he rebuilt his reputation, scoring at an elite rate and leading the club to Süper Lig titles, cementing his status as a cult hero in Istanbul.
