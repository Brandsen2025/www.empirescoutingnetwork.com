Player Overview

José Óscar "Turu" Flores is an Argentine former professional footballer, a powerful and clutch striker who became a legend on two continents. He was a key figure in the most glorious period of Vélez Sarsfield's history, playing under Carlos Bianchi. With Vélez, he won three domestic titles, the 1994 Copa Libertadores, and the 1994 Intercontinental Cup, famously defeating Fabio Capello's AC Milan. He then moved to Spain, first with Las Palmas and then with Deportivo La Coruña, where he was a vital part of the "Super Depor" team that shocked the world to win the 1999–2000 La Liga title.
