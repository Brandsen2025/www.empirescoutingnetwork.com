Player Overview

Franz Beckenbauer redefined modern defending through the attacking libero role, blending defensive intelligence with forward orchestration from the back. Nicknamed "Der Kaiser", he combined elegance, spatial control and leadership, captaining both Bayern Munich and West Germany to their greatest triumphs and later succeeding as a manager and football executive.

Career Timeline

Youth: SC 1906 München (1951–1959). Bayern Munich breakthrough and rise to captain (1964–1977). New York Cosmos spells (1977–1980, 1983–1984). Hamburger SV (1980–1982). International peak: West Germany captain, European Champion 1972 and World Cup winner 1974. Managerial peak: West Germany manager and 1990 World Cup winner.
