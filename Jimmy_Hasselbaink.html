Player Overview

Jerrel "Jimmy" Floyd Hasselbaink was a formidable centre-forward defined by his explosive shooting power (elite STK) and predatory finishing (high GOL/RDS). Possessing great pace (SPR) and strength (PHY), he was a nightmare for defenders, capable of scoring from anywhere in the attacking third. While primarily a goalscorer, he also contributed through his physicality and direct running. He won the Premier League Golden Boot with two different clubs, highlighting his consistent goal threat at the highest level.

  • Directness: Always looked to get a shot away; constant goal threat.

Weaknesses
