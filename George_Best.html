Player Overview

George Best was a footballing genius, arguably the greatest dribbler the sport has seen (elite DXT, FLA, CRK). He possessed a unique combination of blistering pace, perfect balance, two-footedness, and audacious skill, allowing him to beat multiple defenders at will. Beyond his dribbling, he was a prolific goalscorer (high GOL, STK) and a creative force. Part of Manchester United's 'Holy Trinity', his peak was stratospheric, winning the Ballon d'Or and European Cup, though his career was ultimately shortened by his off-field lifestyle.

Strengths and Weaknesses

Strengths

  • Dribbling & Balance (DXT/FLA): Generational talent; could beat any defender.
  • Pace & Agility (SPR): Explosive speed and quick changes of direction.
  • Goalscoring (GOL/STK): Prolific scorer from wide/central roles; great finishing.
  • Two-Footedness: Extremely comfortable and effective with both feet.
  • Creativity (CRK/KEY): High-risk, high-reward player who created numerous chances.

Weaknesses

  • Discipline & Professionalism (CHR): Off-field lifestyle famously cut his peak career short.
  • Defensive Contribution (MRK/TPI): Minimal; purely an attacking force.
  • Temperament: Could be volatile and inconsistent, especially later in career.
  • Physicality: Relied on agility/balance, not brute strength.
