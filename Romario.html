Player Overview

Romário is one of football's all-time pure finishers, defined by lethal penalty‑area instincts, exceptional short‑burst acceleration and an uncanny ability to score from minimal space; World Cup 1994 Golden Ball winner and a defining striker across PSV, Barcelona and Brazil's national team.
