Player Overview

George Weah is one of football's transformative forwards of the 1990s — a rare blend of pace, power and dribbling who won the Ballon d'Or and FIFA World Player of the Year while still representing an African nation. Prolific in France and Italy and decisive on the biggest stages, Weah set a template for multi‑functional strikers who combine individual brilliance with team outcomes and later transitioned to national leadership roles.
