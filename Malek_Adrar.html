Player Overview

Malek Adrar is a young central midfielder with a promising physical profile (1.87m). After developing in France, notably with the Montpellier U19s, he has moved to Belgium to begin his senior career. He spent time with Francs Borains before moving to RFC Seraing in August 2025. He is currently splitting time between the U21 Pro League (where he has a goal and an assist in 3 starts) and the senior Challenger Pro League team. He is a "prospect" focused on translating his youth pedigree into consistent senior minutes.
