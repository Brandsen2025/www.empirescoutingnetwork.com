Player Overview

Juan Riquelme Angulo Caicedo is one of South America's most talked-about striking prospects, emerging from the elite Independiente del Valle academy. Despite his name, he is not a playmaker; he is a dominant 1.93m (6'4") centre-forward. He burst into global focus at the 2024 South American U-15 Championship, where he finished as the tournament's top scorer, netting goals against Brazil and Uruguay. His performances were so impressive that in October 2025, he was listed on The Guardian's prestigious "Next Generation" list as one of the best talents born in 2008. He has already trialed with European clubs and is on the radar of major teams like FC Barcelona.
