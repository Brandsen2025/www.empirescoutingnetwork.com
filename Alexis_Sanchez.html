Player Overview

Alexis Sánchez is a Chilean professional footballer who defines the modern hard-working forward. Famed as "El Niño Maravilla" since his breakout at Cobreloa, he moved to Europe with Udinese before being signed by Pep Guardiola's Barcelona. He found his greatest individual success at Arsenal, becoming one of the Premier League's best players, leading the club to two FA Cups. His career is also marked by leading Chile to their only two Copa América titles (2015, 2016). After a difficult spell at Manchester United, he rebuilt his career at Inter Milan (winning a Serie A title), Marseille, and now Sevilla. His game is a blur of perpetual motion, technical dribbling, and a ferocious desire to win.
