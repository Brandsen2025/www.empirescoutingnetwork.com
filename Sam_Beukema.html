Player Overview

Sam Beukema is a Dutch centre-back whose career is on a sharp upward trajectory. He started his career at Go Ahead Eagles, making his debut in the Eerste Divisie. His dominant performances earned him a move to AZ Alkmaar in 2021, where he became an Eredivisie regular and a standout in the Europa Conference League. In 2023, he made a €10M move to Bologna, where he was a critical part of Thiago Motta's system, leading the team to a historic 2024-25 Coppa Italia victory. A modern, ball-playing defender known for his long passing and defensive awareness, his success led to a major €31M transfer to Napoli in July 2025.
