Player Overview

Profile: Anis Hadj Moussa (b. 11 Feb 2002) is a direct left-footed winger/forward who progressed through French youth systems and lower-league senior steps before signing for Feyenoord in 2024. Combines pace, directness and progressive carries with an improving finishing output at Eredivisie and European level.

Style: Wide forward who attacks half-space lanes, drifts inside on his stronger foot, and times late runs into the box. Effective in transition and in counter-attacking sequences.

Role fit: Best as an inverted right winger in high-tempo systems, useful as a secondary striker in two-forward systems or as a bench-impact wide attacker for rotational depth. Suited to Eredivisie and progressive European teams valuing directness and verticality.
