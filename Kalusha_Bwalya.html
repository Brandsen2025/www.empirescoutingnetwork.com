Player Overview

Kalusha Bwalya was an artist on the ball. His game was not defined by pace but by supreme technical skill and intelligence. He operated as both a creator and a goalscorer, famous for his "magic" left foot which he used for precise passes, spectacular free-kicks, and long-range goals. He famously scored a hat-trick against Italy in the 1988 Seoul Olympics and was the face of Zambian football for over a decade, guiding the national team as player and coach.
