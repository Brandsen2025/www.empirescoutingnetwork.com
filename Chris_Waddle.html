Player Overview

Chris Waddle is a high-velocity, technically brilliant winger and attacking midfielder celebrated for mesmerising dribbles, precise crossing and game-changing creativity; instrumental in Newcastle's rise, Tottenham's attacking periods, Marseille's three consecutive Ligue 1 titles and England's 1986 and 1990 World Cup campaigns.
