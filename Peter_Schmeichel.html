Player Overview

Peter Bolesław Schmeichel was a force of nature who revolutionized goalkeeping. "The Great Dane" was a true "sweeper-keeper" before the term was common, defined by his massive 1.93m frame, his vocal, intimidating leadership (CHR 10.0), and his unparalleled 1-v-1 ability. His iconic "starfish" save (GK_1v1 10.0) made the goal seem tiny. He was the rock behind Denmark's "fairy tale" 1992 Euro win and the on-field captain and emotional heart of Manchester United's 1999 Treble-winning team.
