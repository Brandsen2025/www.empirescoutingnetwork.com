Player Overview

Paulo Silas was a talented and intelligent Brazilian midfielder, known for his creative passing and technical skill. He emerged from the São Paulo academy and quickly became a star, earning a move to Sporting CP in Portugal and later playing in Italy for Cesena and Sampdoria. He was also a prominent member of the Brazil national team, earning 34 caps and playing a role in the 1989 Copa América-winning squad and at the 1990 World Cup, where he wore the #10 jersey.
