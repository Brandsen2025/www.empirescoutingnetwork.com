Player Overview

Ronald Koeman was a unique phenomenon: a defender with the stats of an elite striker. As the highest-scoring defender in history (253 goals), his game was built on a "legendarily hard" shot (STK 10.0) and "pinpoint passing". A product of "Total Football," he was a complete player, excelling as a ball-playing sweeper (libero) who could orchestrate the attack from deep and score decisive goals, most famously the 1992 European Cup-winning free-kick for Barcelona.
