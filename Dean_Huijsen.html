Player Overview

Dean Huijsen is a unique, "ball-playing defender" who embodies the qualities of a modern centre-back. His towering 1.97m frame gives him "aerial dominance," but his defining trait is his "exceptional composure on the ball." He is an aggressive, front-foot defender and an elite progressive passer, often resembling a "quarterback" with his ability to play "looping passes in behind." He is also a penalty specialist. After breakthrough loans at Roma and a permanent move to Bournemouth, Real Madrid triggered his £50m release clause in mid-2025. Born in the Netherlands, he now represents the Spain national team.
