Player Overview

Jhon Arias is an explosive and unpredictable attacking midfielder, primarily operating as a winger who is comfortable on either flank. His game is defined by his 1-v-1 dominance, able to beat multiple defenders with a low center of gravity and exceptional agility (high AGI, high DRI). After winning a Colombian title with América de Cali, he became a superstar at Fluminense, winning the 2023 Copa Libertadores and the 2024 Recopa Sudamericana. He was named in the Team of the Tournament for the Libertadores and the Club World Cup, leading to a 2025 transfer to Wolves in the Premier League.
