Player Overview

Pelé (Edson Arantes do Nascimento) represents the absolute pinnacle of footballing completeness. He combined the goalscoring record of an elite striker (GOL, RDS) with the creativity of a No. 10 (KEY, SCA, FLA), the athleticism of a top athlete (SPR, PHY, DXT), and the intelligence of a master tactician (POI, DMA). He was dominant with both feet, superb in the air despite his height, and possessed a winning mentality (CHR, CRS) that led his teams to three World Cups and countless club honors. His impact on the game is immeasurable.
