Club overview

Rosario Central is a historic Argentine club known for blue-and-gold identity, derby intensity, pioneering lineage, and a unique post‑promotion league title. Central’s competitive DNA blends expressive play with pragmatic edge, supported by one of South America’s most imposing home atmospheres at the Gigante de Arroyito.
