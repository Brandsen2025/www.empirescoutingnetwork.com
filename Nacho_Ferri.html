Player Overview

Nacho Ferri is a classic target man, defined by his "huge" 1.92m frame and exceptional physical presence. His primary strength is using his body to hold off defenders, protect the ball, and dominate aerial duels. After a prolific spell with Eintracht Frankfurt II (52 apps, 36 goals) and a successful loan at KV Kortrijk (32 apps, 8 goals), he secured a permanent transfer to KVC Westerlo in July 2025. He is a former Spain U19 international.
