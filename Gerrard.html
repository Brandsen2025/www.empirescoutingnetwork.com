Player Overview

Steven Gerrard is one of the defining midfielders of the modern age — a relentless box‑to‑box leader combining timing, range of passing, late‑arrival goals and match‑winning mentality. A driving force at Liverpool for 17 seasons, Gerrard produced decisive moments across domestic and European competitions and transitioned into management with a pragmatic, standards-first coaching approach.
