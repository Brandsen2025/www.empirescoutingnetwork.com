Career Overview

Lionel Messi is an Argentine professional footballer who is widely considered the greatest player of his generation and arguably of all time. His career is a testament to unparalleled consistency, technical perfection, and relentless goal-scoring. From dominating Europe with Barcelona to finally achieving international glory with Argentina at the World Cup, Messi's legacy is defined by breaking nearly every conceivable record in the sport.
