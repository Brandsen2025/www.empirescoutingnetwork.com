Player Overview

Mario Alberto Kempes Chiodi, "El Matador," was the complete striker of his generation, blending raw power (PHY 9.8) with prolific goalscoring (GOL 10.0) and a relentless work ethic. He was not a traditional poacher; his trademark was gathering the ball deep and embarking on powerful, surging runs (DXT 9.2), culminating in ferocious left-footed shots (SHO 9.7). His performance in the 1978 World Cup is legendary, carrying Argentina to victory with a combination of goals, leadership, and an unstoppable will to win (CHR 9.9).
