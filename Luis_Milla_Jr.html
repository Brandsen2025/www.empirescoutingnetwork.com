Player Overview

Luis Milla Manzanares is a Spanish professional midfielder who has successfully navigated the immense pressure of his family name. A "late bloomer," he forged his own path through Spain's lower divisions with clubs like Fuenlabrada and Guijuelo. His career took off at CD Tenerife, where his elite passing and vision earned him a move to La Liga with Granada. He scored on his top-flight debut and became a key player. In 2022, he joined Getafe, where he serves as the team's primary creative "brain" and set-piece specialist.
