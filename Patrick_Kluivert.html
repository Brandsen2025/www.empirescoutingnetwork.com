Player Overview

Patrick Kluivert is a Dutch football legend, a "total footballer" in the #9 position. He exploded onto the world scene as an 18-year-old, coming off the bench to score the 85th-minute winning goal for Ajax in the 1995 UEFA Champions League Final. He was a complete striker, combining a tall, powerful 1.88m frame with the "elite" technique, intelligence, and first touch of the Ajax academy. After a year at AC Milan, he reunited with Louis van Gaal at Barcelona, where he became a club legend, scoring 122 goals. He was also a superstar for the Netherlands, earning a spot on the 1998 World Cup All-Star Team and winning the Euro 2000 Golden Boot.
