Player Overview

Paolo Maldini is a generational defender whose career at AC Milan and Italy set the modern defensive standard. Equally effective as an attacking left-back in his early years and as a composed centre-back later, Maldini combined exceptional reading of the game, technical elegance and sustained leadership across 25 seasons. Five-time European champion and long-time captain, his reliability, versatility and elite positioning define a near-perfect defensive profile.
