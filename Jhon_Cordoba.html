Player Overview

Jhon Córdoba is a complete, modern target man. He combines an elite physical frame (1.88m, 85kg) with impressive finishing and a high defensive work rate. After a 2013 South American Youth Championship win with Colombia, he had a long career in Europe with Espanyol and Granada before starring in Germany for Mainz and 1. FC Köln, where he scored 37 goals in 86 games. He is now the star striker for FC Krasnodar (64 goals in 133 apps) and a regular #9 for the Colombia national team.
