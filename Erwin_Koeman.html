Player Overview

Erwin Koeman carved out an impressive career as an international-level centre-back, standing as the middle brother between Martin and the legendary Ronald Koeman. A solid, dependable defender with excellent positional sense (POI: 8.5) and strong tackling ability (TAC: 8.4), Erwin enjoyed his peak years at PSV Eindhoven where he played alongside his younger brother Ronald. Together, they anchored PSV's defence during the club's greatest era, winning the treble in 1988 including the European Cup. Erwin was also part of Netherlands' Euro 1988 championship squad, earning the right to be called a European champion. With 31 caps for the Netherlands and an 18-year professional career, Erwin proved himself a quality international defender who excelled in multiple championship-winning teams. While overshadowed by Ronald's legendary status, Erwin's career represents a successful international defender who won major honours at the highest level.
