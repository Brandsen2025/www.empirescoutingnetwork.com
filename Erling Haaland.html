Player Overview

Erling Haaland is a generational centre-forward defined by blend of size, first-step acceleration and clinical finishing; elite at occupying penalty box channels, timing blindside runs and converting varied chances at extreme efficiency, delivering historic scoring rates at Salzburg, Dortmund and Manchester City.
