Player Overview

Stefan Bajcetic is a Spanish-Serbian footballer who represents the modern, technical "pivot" midfielder. Signed by Liverpool from Celta Vigo's academy in 2020 as a centre-back, his football intelligence saw him retrained as a defensive midfielder. He made a stunning breakthrough at just 18 in the 2022-23 season, becoming a regular starter and a rare bright spot in a difficult campaign for the team. His ability to receive the ball under pressure, combined with clean tackling and sharp passing, drew comparisons to Sergio Busquets. A complex adductor injury sidelined him for nearly all of the 2023-24 season, and he is now focused on rebuilding his fitness to fulfill his world-class potential.
