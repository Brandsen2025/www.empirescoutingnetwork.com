Player Overview

Virgil van Dijk is a generational central defender who transformed Liverpool's defence after his 2018 arrival. He pairs elite physical attributes with refined positional intelligence and exceptional passing for a centre-back. A natural leader and proven winner at club and international level, Van Dijk's influence extends from defensive organisation to initiating attacks with progressive, line-breaking passes.
