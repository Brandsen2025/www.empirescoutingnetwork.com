Player Overview

Karl-Heinz "Kalle" Rummenigge was a complete and powerful forward, the dominant striker of his generation. He blended blistering pace (PAC 9.4) and power (PHY 9.5) with exceptional dribbling ability (DXT 9.7) and lethal, prolific finishing (GOL 9.8). He was a talismanic leader (CHR 9.5) for both club and country, winning back-to-back Ballon d'Or awards in 1980 and 1981. He was the driving force behind Bayern's two European Cups and West Germany's 1980 Euro triumph.
