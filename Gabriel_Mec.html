Player Overview

Gabriel Mec is the latest phenom to emerge from Grêmio's academy, a player widely considered the most exciting striker in Brazil's 2008 generation. His background is unique: he is a taekwondo black belt with Olympic potential who chose football. This athletic base is evident in his play, which is defined by explosive speed, balance, and "intoxicating" dribbling ability. He is a two-footed forward who "combines the best characteristics of Brazilian football." His "immense talent" is no secret, with Grêmio already turning down significant €15m offers from European giants like Chelsea.
