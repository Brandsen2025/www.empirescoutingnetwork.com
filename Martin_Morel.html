Player Overview

Martín Morel was a technical and prolific attacking midfielder. He was a "late developer," joining Tigre at 25 and becoming the creative engine that led them to promotion and an improbable runner-up finish in the 2008 Apertura, where he scored 13 goals. He was renowned for his finishing (high FIN), vision (high VIS), and technical flair (high TEC). His peak continued with a spectacular, short-lived spell at Deportivo Cali in 2010, where he scored 11 goals in 14 games, including a hat-trick (with a bicycle kick) in the derby vs. América de Cali.
