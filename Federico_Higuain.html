Player Overview

Federico "Pipa" Higuaín is an Argentine former professional footballer and a legend of Major League Soccer. The older brother of Gonzalo, Federico was a classic "enganche" or #10, defined by his sublime vision, creativity, and technical elegance. After a nomadic early career (River Plate, Beşiktaş, Club América), he found his home at the Columbus Crew in 2012. He immediately won the MLS Newcomer of the Year and became the club's talisman, setting the franchise record for all-time assists. He was a fan favorite known for his flair, intelligence, and leadership, ending his career playing alongside his brother at Inter Miami.
