Player Overview

Henrik "Henke" Larsson is a Swedish legend, best known as the "King of Kings" at Celtic, where he became the club's third all-time top scorer with 242 goals in 315 games. His 2000-01 season was one of the greatest in history, as he scored 53 goals in all competitions to win the European Golden Boot. A complete striker, he blended intelligent movement, lethal finishing, and aerial prowess. After Celtic, he proved his elite quality at Barcelona, famously coming off the bench in the 2006 Champions League final to provide both assists in the 2-1 comeback win over Arsenal. He also won 2 La Liga titles and a Premier League title with Manchester United. For Sweden, he earned 106 caps and won a 1994 World Cup bronze medal.
