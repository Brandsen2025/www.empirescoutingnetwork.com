Player Overview

Claudio "El Sifón" Ubeda was the quintessential Argentine captain. A tough, intelligent, and fiercely committed left-footed centre-back, his legend is not defined by skill (though he was a solid defender) but by his immense leadership. He joined Racing Club during a period of deep financial and institutional crisis and became the on-field symbol of their resilience. His career peaked in 2001 when he captained "Mostaza" Merlo's team to the Apertura title, breaking a 35-year curse. That victory made him an eternal idol for the club, embodying the "step-by-step" (paso a paso) mentality of that championship team.
