Player Overview

Jozy Altidore, at his peak, was a dominant physical striker, embodying the **Power Target Forward** archetype. His game was built on elite strength (PHY), excellent hold-up play, and powerful finishing (STK, GOL). He served as the focal point of the attack, occupying defenders, winning aerial duels, and bringing teammates into play. While his time in some European leagues was challenging (e.g., EPL), he was prolific in the Eredivisie and became a legend for Toronto FC and the USMNT by scoring crucial, clutch goals (high CRS) in finals.
