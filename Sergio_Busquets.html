Player Overview

Sergio Busquets Burgos is one of the most intelligent and decorated footballers of all time. Famously described by Vicente del Bosque: "You watch the game, you don't see Busquets. You watch Busquets, you see the whole game." He is the definitive "Pivot," a player who relies on zero athletic attributes and 100% on his brain. His ability to anticipate play, his flawless defensive positioning, his "one-touch" passing, and his press resistance made him the single most important tactical piece for both Guardiola's Barcelona and Spain's 2010 World Cup-winning side. He is the ultimate "system" player, a quiet genius who made everyone around him look better.
