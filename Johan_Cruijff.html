Player Overview

Johan Cruyff reshaped modern football as the embodiment of Total Football: a player-manager whose technical brilliance, spatial intelligence and managerial philosophy created tactical blueprints still used worldwide; three-time Ballon d'Or winner and catalytic figure for Ajax and Barcelona.
