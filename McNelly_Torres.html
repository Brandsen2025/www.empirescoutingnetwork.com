Quick Summary

A quintessential Colombian playmaker, Macnelly Torres was the creative engine and "brain" of the dominant Atlético Nacional team that won the 2016 Copa Libertadores. Renowned for his tactical intelligence, exceptional vision, and precise passing, "El Mago" was a master of the 'pausa' (the pause) and controlling game tempo.

Player Overview

Macnelly Torres was one of the most intelligent and effective playmakers in South American football during his peak. His game was not defined by athleticism but by his mind (high IQ, high VIS), his technique (high TEC), and his flawless passing (high PAS). He was the creative fulcrum for Atlético Nacional's 2016 Copa Libertadores victory, dictating play and providing key assists. He also won league titles in Colombia (with Cúcuta, Junior, and Nacional), Chile (Colo-Colo), and Saudi Arabia (Al-Shabab), cementing his status as a serial winner.
