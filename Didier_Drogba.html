Player Overview

Didier Yves Drogba Tébily is a Chelsea and Ivory Coast legend, widely regarded as one of the most dominant and "clutch" strikers of the 21st century. After prolific spells at Guingamp and Marseille, he joined Chelsea in 2004 and became the focal point of a dynasty. He scored 164 goals for the club, winning 4 Premier League titles and 2 Golden Boots. His power, aerial ability, and unshakeable mentality made him the "ultimate big game player," scoring in a record 4 FA Cup finals and, most famously, scoring the 88th-minute equalizer and the winning penalty to win the 2012 Champions League. He is also the all-time top scorer for the Ivory Coast (65 goals).
