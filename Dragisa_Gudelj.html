Quick Summary

Son of Nebojša and brother of Nemanja. Dragiša is a physical, dedicated Serbian defender known for his exceptional **Clutch Response Score (CRS)** and resilience. His career is uniquely defined by his return to professional football after suffering two on-field cardiac arrests and receiving an ICD (Implantable Cardioverter-Defibrillator).
