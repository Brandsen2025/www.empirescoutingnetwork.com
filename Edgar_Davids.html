Player Overview

Edgar "The Pitbull" Davids was a force of nature in midfield, blending relentless energy and ferocious tackling (elite TPI/MRK) with impressive close control and dribbling ability (high DXT). His stamina allowed him to cover immense ground, contributing significantly in both defensive and attacking phases. A key component of multiple trophy-winning sides, his aggressive ball-winning and dynamic presence defined the box-to-box midfielder role for his generation.
