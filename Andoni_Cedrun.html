Player Overview

Andoni Cedrún is a Spanish goalkeeper and a "historical" legend of Real Zaragoza. Son of the great Athletic Club keeper Carmelo Cedrún, Andoni began his career at his father's club before moving to Zaragoza in 1983. It was there he became an icon, making a club-record 403 appearances over 13 seasons. He was a charismatic and unorthodox keeper, known for his spectacular reflexes and big-game mentality. He was the starting #1 for Zaragoza's "golden generation," famously winning the 1994 Copa del Rey and the 1995 UEFA Cup Winners' Cup in a dramatic final against Arsenal.
