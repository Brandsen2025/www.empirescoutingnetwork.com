Player Overview

Ruud Gullit (born Rudi Dil) was a revolutionary player, the perfect blend of athletic power and technical grace. His dreadlocked figure was iconic, but his game was even more so. He was a true "Total Footballer," capable of dominating as a forward, an attacking midfielder, or even a sweeper. He possessed blinding pace, immense strength (PHY 10.0), tireless stamina, and an elite footballing brain (POI 9.8). He was a leader, a prolific goalscorer, and a creator, winning the 1987 Ballon d'Or and captaining the Netherlands to glory at Euro 1988.
