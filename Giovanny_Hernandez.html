Quick Summary

Player Overview

Giovanny "El Príncipe" Hernández was one of the most talented playmakers of his generation. A classic #10, his game was defined by superb vision (high VIS), precise passing (high PAS), and an elegant first touch (high TEC). He was the creative engine for every club he played for, most notably becoming a legend at Atlético Junior, where he scored 52 goals in 203 appearances. He was also a star in Argentina with Colón and a key member of the Colombia squad that won the 2001 Copa América.
