Player Overview

Roberto "Chorri" Palacios was the creative heartbeat of Sporting Cristal and Peru for over a decade. Defined by superb technical skill, close control, vision, and a penchant for decisive moments (high CRK, FLA, KEY), he operated primarily as an attacking midfielder or winger. His ability to unlock defenses with dribbles, through balls, and powerful long shots made him a constant threat and a national icon. A leader through skill and determination (high CHR).
