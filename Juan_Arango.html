Player Overview

Juan Arango was the quintessential "luxury" playmaker, a technical artist defined by his magical left foot. His game was built on supreme elegance (ELE), vision (KEY), and arguably the best set-piece delivery (STK) in the world during his peak. He dictated play with a wide array of passes (LGP) and was a constant threat to score from distance. While lacking in pace (SPR) and defensive work rate (TPI/MRK), his on-ball genius made him an icon for Gladbach, Mallorca, and the undisputed talisman for the Venezuelan national team for over a decade.

Weaknesses
