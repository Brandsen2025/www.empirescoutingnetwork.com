Player Overview

Denis Law, 'The King' of Old Trafford, was a truly electrifying forward. He combined predatory goalscoring instincts (elite GOL, RDS) with exceptional flair (FLA), agility, and a penchant for the spectacular, particularly acrobatic finishes (high STK). Despite not being tall, his timing and leap made him a superb aerial threat. Fiercely competitive (CHR) and possessing quick feet (DXT) and sharp movement (POI), Law was a complete attacker who terrorized defenses and remains one of football's all-time great goalscorers.
