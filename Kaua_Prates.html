Quick Summary

One of the "jewels of Brazilian football," Prates is a supremely versatile and modern athlete. Player Overview

Kauã Prates is a remarkable prospect from the Cruzeiro academy, standing out for his elite versatility and physical/technical blend. Able to play as a left-back, centre-back, or winger, he has been a fixture in Brazil's age-group teams since he was 14. His style draws comparisons to former Milan and Brazil star Serginho, highlighting his mix of "quality and strength." The high esteem he is held in is best summarized by his U-17 coach, former Monaco manager Leonardo Jardim, who said he has such quality that he would have signed him for Monaco before he even turned 18.
