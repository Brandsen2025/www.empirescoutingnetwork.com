Player Overview

Jude Bellingham is a truly complete midfielder, excelling in nearly every facet of the game at an elite level despite his youth. He combines the technical skill and creativity of an attacking midfielder (DXT, KEY, GOL) with the engine and defensive contribution of a box-to-box player (DYN, TPI, MRK, INT) and the intelligence and leadership of a seasoned veteran (POI, DMA, CHR). His ability to drive forward with the ball, arrive late in the box to score, win duels, and dictate play marks him as a generational talent.
