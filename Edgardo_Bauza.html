Player Overview

Edgardo "El Patón" Bauza was a unique phenomenon: a centre-back with the goalscoring record of a forward (remarkably high GOL for position). His game was defined by immense physical presence (high PHY), unparalleled aerial ability in both boxes, strong defensive fundamentals (high MRK/POI), and leadership (high CHR). He was a constant threat on set pieces and penalties, making him one of the highest-impact defenders of his generation, particularly in South American football.

Strengths and Weaknesses

Strengths

  • Goalscoring (GOL): Extraordinary goal threat for a defender, especially from set pieces and penalties.
  • Aerial Dominance: Elite heading ability in both defense and attack.
  • Physicality (PHY): Tall (1.89m), strong, imposing presence.
  • Leadership (CHR): Natural leader, commanded the defense.
  • Defensive Fundamentals (MRK/POI): Solid positioning and marking ability.

Weaknesses

  • Pace (SPR): Not exceptionally quick; relied on positioning and reading play.
  • Technical Skill (On Ball): Functional, but not a refined ball-playing defender by modern standards.
  • Agility: Less mobile against quick, tricky attackers.
