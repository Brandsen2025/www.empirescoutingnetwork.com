Bio & quick facts
Pioneering keeper who redefined the role: first glove user in Argentina, regularly left his area, and used goal kicks to start attacks. Long River Plate career (1945–1968) and final years at Millonarios; IFFHS-ranked among South America's best of the 20th century.
