Player Overview

Igor Dobrovolski was a technically excellent Soviet midfielder renowned for his creative vision, precise passing, and tactical intelligence. Operating primarily as an attacking midfielder, he possessed the ability to control games with his distribution, set-piece expertise, and intelligent movement between the lines. His versatility allowed him to play in various midfield roles while maintaining high creative output.
