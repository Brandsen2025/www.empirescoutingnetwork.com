Player Overview

George Best combined extraordinary dribbling, improvisation and finishing to become one of football's most magnetic attackers. A generational talent at Manchester United, Best's peak (mid‑1960s) included domestic titles and European Cup success; his career arc mixes football genius and the well-documented impact of lifestyle on longevity and availability.

Career Timeline

Youth: Cregagh Boys Club; Manchester United youth and first team breakthrough (1963–1974). Post‑United journeyman period across UK, USA, Australia and Ireland (1974–1984). International: Northern Ireland (1964–1977). Transitioned to media and ambassador roles post-retirement; passed away 2005.
