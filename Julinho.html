Player Overview

Julinho was a fast, skillful, and entertaining Brazilian forward who became a legend in Peru. After playing for several clubs in Brazil, he moved to Peru's Defensor Lima in 1991. He was quickly signed by Sporting Cristal in 1993, where he became an icon. He was the star of their dominant "Tri-Campeón" team (1994, 1995, 1996) and the 1997 Copa Libertadores runner-up squad. A classic Brazilian winger, his game was built on flair (high FLA), dribbling (high DRI), and pace (high PAC). He became a naturalized citizen and played 12 times for the Peru national team.
