Player Overview

Thomás de Martis is arguably the most exciting striking prospect in Argentine football. Born in Mar del Plata, his life is "all about goals," earning him the nickname "killer" and comparisons to a young Lautaro Martínez. His 2025 has been explosive: he starred at the prestigious COTIF tournament (scoring against Chile, Brazil, and Valencia) and has a phenomenal record of 11 goals in 13 games for the Argentina U-17 national team. Already training with the first team under Mauricio Pellegrino and having made his top-flight debut, he is on a fast track to stardom. As Lanús president Nicolás Russo stated, "Thomás is the most exciting prospect in Argentinian football."
