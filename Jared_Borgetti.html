Player Overview

Jared "El Zorro del Desierto" Borgetti was a specialist finisher of the highest order, defined by his generational aerial ability. While not blessed with exceptional pace or dribbling skills, his game was built on elite intelligence inside the box (POI, RDS) and arguably the best heading technique (STK, Aerials 10.0) of his era. He was a master of timing his runs and adjusting his body to meet crosses, most famously demonstrated by his iconic looping header against Italy in the 2002 World Cup. A legend for Santos Laguna and Mexico.
