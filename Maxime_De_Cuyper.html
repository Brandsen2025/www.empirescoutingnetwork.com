Player Overview

Maxime de Cuyper represents the new generation of Belgian full-backs, blending tactical intelligence with an elite engine. A product of the famed Club Brugge academy, he was battle-hardened during a highly successful two-year loan at KVC Westerlo, where he was a key figure in their promotion and subsequent success. He returned to Brugge in 2023 as a mature, complete player, immediately becoming the first-choice left-back, winning the Pro League title, and earning a call-up to the Belgian national team for EURO 2024. His game is defined by relentless running, high-volume crossing, and sound defensive positioning.

Strengths and Weaknesses

Strengths

  • Stamina & Work Rate: Elite. A "machine" who covers the entire flank for 90 minutes.
  • Crossing: High-volume, accurate crosser from open play and set pieces.
  • Offensive Output: Excellent goal and assist numbers for a full-back.
  • Tactical Intelligence: Good positioning, understands when to overlap and when to hold.
  • Versatility: Can play as a traditional LB, an attacking LWB, or even as a RWB.

Weaknesses

  • 1v1 Defending: Solid and reliable, but can be beaten by elite, tricky wingers.
  • Top-End Pace: More of a "long-distance runner" than an explosive sprinter.
  • Physical Strength: Not physically imposing, can be outmuscled in duels.

Global Impact Analysis

Brugge's New Guard: De Cuyper is a pillar of the new generation at Club Brugge, a club that has become a dominant force in Belgium and a consistent European competitor. His successful loan is a model for player development.

Belgian Red Devil: He is a key part of the post-"Golden Generation" rebuild for the Belgian national team. Under Domenico Tedesco, his high-energy, modern full-back profile has been favored, and he projects as a long-term solution for the left-back position.

Core Tactical DNA

Playing Style: A "high-volume" attacking wing-back. His DNA is built on an elite engine, allowing him to be his team's primary source of width for 90 minutes. He constantly overlaps, delivering a high number of crosses into the box. He is not just an attacker, however; his work rate ensures he recovers his defensive position reliably. He is a modern, all-around full-back who excels in systems that demand extreme fitness from the wide defenders.
