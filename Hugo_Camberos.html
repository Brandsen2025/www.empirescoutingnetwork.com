Player Overview

Hugo Camberos is a technically gifted and pacy winger emerging from the Chivas Guadalajara academy. His game is built on his 1v1 ability (DXT, FLA) and willingness to take on defenders (CRK). As a dual-national (USA/Mexico) who has represented Mexico at youth levels, he is a high-potential prospect gaining crucial professional experience on loan at CD Tapatío. While his end product (GOL, KEY) is still developing, his raw speed (SPR) and technical foundation make him one to watch.
