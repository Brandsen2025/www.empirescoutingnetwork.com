Player Overview

Kendry Páez is one of the most exciting and creative young talents in world football. A classic left-footed #10, he plays with a "magical" flair, defined by his exceptional dribbling (high DRI), 1-v-1 dominance, and the vision to open up defenses with "excellent through balls" (high VIS). After breaking every "youngest-ever" record at Independiente del Valle and for the Ecuador national team, he was signed by Chelsea for a record €20M fee. He is currently developing on loan at RC Strasbourg for the 2025-26 season.
