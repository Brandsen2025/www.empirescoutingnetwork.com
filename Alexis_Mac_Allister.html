Player Overview

Alexis Mac Allister is a world-class Argentine central midfielder, a key member of the 2022 World Cup and 2024 Copa América winning squads. The son of former Boca defender Carlos Mac Allister, Alexis began his career at Argentinos Juniors before starring at Boca Juniors. He moved to Brighton, where he developed into a Premier League star, before a major transfer to Liverpool. He is the complete modern midfielder: a "Guardiola Positional" player with the "Simeone Cholismo" of his father. He is technically elite, a "tireless" worker, highly press-resistant, and tactically versatile, capable of playing as a #6, #8, or #10.
