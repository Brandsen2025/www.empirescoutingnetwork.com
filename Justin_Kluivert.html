Player Overview

Justin Kluivert is a Dutch international forward who plays for Premier League club Bournemouth. The son of legend Patrick Kluivert, he is a graduate of the famed Ajax academy. He burst onto the scene with a 10-goal season for Ajax, earning a move to AS Roma. A true "journeyman," he became one of the few players to compete in all of Europe's "Big 5" leagues with loans to RB Leipzig, OGC Nice, and Valencia. He found stability at Bournemouth in 2023, where his "elite" pace and direct dribbling have made him a key attacker. He recently won the PL Player of the Month (Nov 2024).
