Player Overview

Rogério Ceni is a singular figure in football, defined by his status as the highest-scoring goalkeeper of all time (131 official goals). Beyond this unique feat, achieved through exceptional free-kick and penalty technique (elite STK), he was a highly accomplished goalkeeper with solid fundamentals (SAV, positioning), longevity, and immense leadership qualities (high CHR). A one-club legend for São Paulo, captaining them to numerous domestic and international titles.
