Player Overview

David Crespo Zurita is a Spanish professional footballer from Palma de Mallorca. He plays as a right-back, where his game is defined by "rapidez y fuerza" (speed and strength). After developing in the youth ranks of CD San Francisco U19, he built a long and resilient career in the tough lower divisions of Spanish football, representing clubs such as UE Llagostera, Formentera, and SCR Penya Deportiva. In January 2023, he signed with Andorran powerhouse FC Santa Coloma, becoming a regular starter and helping the team compete in the UEFA Conference League qualifiers.
