Player Overview

Óscar Álvarez Sanjuán is a Spanish former professional footballer. Like his father (Quique Costas) and brother (Quique Álvarez), he was a defender trained at FC Barcelona's La Masia. While he never broke into the Barça first team, he built a long and highly respectable career as a "no-nonsense" central defender. He was a key player for Real Oviedo in La Liga and later became a fixture for CD Tenerife, amassing over 100 appearances for both clubs. He was a solid, reliable, and professional defender.
