Player Overview

Francisco "Paco" Gento is, simply, "Mr. Real Madrid." His trophy haul is mythological: 6 European Cups (the most of any player in history) and 12 La Liga titles (also a record). Nicknamed "La Galerna del Cantábrico" (The Cantabrian Gale), he was a force of nature. His game was built on "supernatural" pace (reportedly running 100m in 11 seconds) and relentless stamina. He was the "outlet" for the 1950s dynasty, a direct, hard-working winger who supplied Di Stéfano and Puskás. He then "evolved" to become the captain and leader of the 1966 "Yé-yé" team, winning his 6th European Cup as the "grizzled" veteran.
