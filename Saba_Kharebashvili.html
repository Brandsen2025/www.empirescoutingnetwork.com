Player Overview

Saba Kharebashvili is a Georgian footballer who is fast becoming one of Europe's most watched defensive prospects. A 1.84m, left-footed player, he possesses a rare and valuable tactical flexibility, excelling as a technical left-back, a ball-playing centre-back, and an intelligent defensive midfielder. After rising through the famed Dinamo Tbilisi academy, he was fast-tracked to the senior team in 2024 at age 16. He immediately became a regular starter, accumulating a massive amount of senior experience (over 50 games) before his 18th birthday. His composure, technique, and reading of the game have led to comparisons with Italian defender Riccardo Calafiori. He made his senior debut for the Georgia national team in June 2025.
