Player Overview

Filip Bundgaard is one of Denmark's brightest attacking prospects. He is a technically gifted playmaker, comfortable operating centrally as a #10 or from either wing. His game is defined by his excellent 1-v-1 dribbling (high DRI), quick feet (high ELE), and ability to both score (high FIN) and create (high VIS). After becoming the youngest-ever player for Randers FC, he made a significant domestic transfer to Brøndby IF in February 2024, where he is seen as a cornerstone player.
