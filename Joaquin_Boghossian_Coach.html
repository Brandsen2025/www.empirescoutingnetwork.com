Managerial Overview

Joaquín Boghossian is a high-potential young manager who has rapidly progressed through the Uruguayan coaching ranks. Immediately after retiring as a player in March 2021, he became an assistant coach. He earned his first head coach role in April 2023 with Uruguay Montevideo and had a very successful stint (1.93 PPG). After a brief, difficult spell in the Primera División with Deportivo Maldonado, he was appointed manager of Albion FC in June 2024. He employs a modern, attacking 4-3-3 philosophy.
