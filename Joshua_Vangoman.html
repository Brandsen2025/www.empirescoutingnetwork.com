Player Overview

Josha Vagnoman is a physically dominant full-back whose primary assets are his explosive pace (SPR) and power (PHY). At 1.90m, he combines the size of a centre-back with the speed of a winger, making him a formidable presence on the right flank. He excels at carrying the ball progressively (high Prog. Carries) and taking on opponents (high Take-Ons for a FB). While his final ball and defensive positioning (POI) are still refining, his athletic potential fits perfectly with high-intensity, transitional systems. Availability due to injuries remains his biggest developmental hurdle.
