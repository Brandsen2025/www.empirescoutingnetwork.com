Player Overview

Santiago Londoño González is a 17-year-old Colombian striker currently playing for Envigado FC. Hailing from the same Medellín Comuna 13 as Juan Fernando Quintero, Londoño is a product of the famed Envigado academy and is considered one of the next great talents to emerge from it. He made his professional debut in 2023 and has already played over 20 senior matches. He gained national attention as the prolific goalscorer for the Colombia U-17 team, scoring 5 goals in the Sudamericano U-17.
