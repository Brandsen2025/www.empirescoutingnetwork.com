Player Overview

Samuel Eto'o Fils is, without question, one of the most decorated and lethal strikers of the modern era. After emerging at RCD Mallorca (70 goals, a club record), he became a global superstar at Barcelona, scoring 130 goals in 199 appearances. There, he won 3 La Liga titles and 2 Champions Leagues, scoring in the 2006 and 2009 finals. In 2009, he moved to Inter Milan and achieved the unthinkable: winning a second consecutive continental treble. A 4-time African Player of the Year, he is also Cameroon's all-time top scorer (56 goals) and an Olympic Gold Medalist (2000).
