Player Overview

Samuel Eto'o is a generational centre‑forward whose blend of explosive acceleration, surgical finishing and tactical intelligence made him one of the most effective strikers of his era. A decisive presence in Champions League finals and continental tournaments, Eto'o combined elite movement, clinical instinct and relentless competitive drive across Spain, Italy and international football.
