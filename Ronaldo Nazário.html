Player Overview

Ronaldo Nazário, nicknamed O Fenômeno, redefined the modern number 9 with unmatched blend of pace, technique and finishing; a generational striker who combined explosive first steps, body feints and clinical composure to dominate PSV, Barcelona, Inter and Brazil's World Cup success in 2002.
