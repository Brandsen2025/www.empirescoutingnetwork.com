Player Overview

Telasco Segovia is a high-potential central midfielder whose game is built on technical security and tactical intelligence. He excels at controlling the tempo from deep, resisting pressure with quick feet and calm decision-making (high CPX, DXT). After standout youth international performances (Toulon '22, Pre-Olympic '24) and difficult European loans (Sampdoria, Casa Pia/Leiria), he has made a high-impact move to Inter Miami in 2025, showing an unexpected goalscoring touch with 7 goals in his first 27 MLS games.
