Player Overview

Khéphren Thuram is a French international midfielder who has emerged as one of the most unique and dominant talents in Europe. The younger son of Lilian Thuram, he is a physical colossus at 1.92m, yet he plays with the grace and technical skill of a much smaller player. His "generational" strength is his ball-carrying; he is one of the world's best at dribbling from deep in midfield to break the press and launch attacks. After developing at AS Monaco, he became a superstar at OGC Nice, earning a spot in the 2022-23 Ligue 1 Team of the Year. In 2024, he completed a €40M move to Juventus, following in his father's footsteps.
