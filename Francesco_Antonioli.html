Legendary Italian goalkeeper with 20-year career (1992-2012) across six top European clubs. Won Serie A Scudetto 2000-01 with AS Roma, making 145 total appearances for Giallorossi (102 league), crucial to their first title in 18 years. Accumulated 416 Serie A matches with 116 clean sheets across career. Renowned penalty specialist: saved 14 spot-kicks, ranking 7th in Serie A history. Part of Italy's Euro 2000 squad (final runners-up), promoted to second-choice after Buffon injury. Won U21 Euro 1992 as first-choice GK + Olympic participation 1992. Also triumphed with AC Milan (2 Serie A, 2 European Cups as squad member), Bologna (Serie B 1995-96, Intertoto 1998). Became oldest Serie A player at Cesena before 2012 retirement. Known for exceptional longevity, professionalism, and shot-stopping consistency.
