Player Overview

Otto Tiittinen is a Finnish professional footballer regarded as one of the top talents of his generation. A 1.84m, left-footed central midfielder, he is a product of the Ilves academy. After debuting for the reserve team in 2024, he signed his first professional contract in January 2025. He made a spectacular Veikkausliiga debut at age 16, scoring the winning goal against HJK Helsinki. Praised for his elite technique, passing, vision, and composure, he was named to *The Guardian's* "Next Generation 2025" list. He is also the captain of the Finland U17 national team.
