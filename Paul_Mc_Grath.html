Player Overview

Paul McGrath is an Irish football legend, revered as "God" by Aston Villa fans for his almost supernatural defensive ability. He was a footballing miracle: a player who, due to chronic, debilitating knee injuries, barely trained but would deliver 10/10 performances on match day. He began his career at Manchester United, winning an FA Cup in 1985. After being sold by Alex Ferguson, he found his home at Aston Villa, where he became a club icon, winning the PFA Players' Player of the Year in 1993 and two League Cups. His performance for Ireland against Italy in the 1994 World Cup, while battling a virus and his knee issues, is considered one of the most heroic individual displays in the tournament's history.
