Career Overview

Dejan Stanković was a dynamic, complete midfielder whose FGA is driven by his elite **Leadership (LEA)** and ability to influence games with powerful long-range efforts (**SHOT 9.8**). He achieved the ultimate club success by winning the Treble with Inter Milan. His strong psychological profile now defines his managerial style, emphasizing high work rate and mental toughness, ensuring he is a highly impactful figure in both careers.
