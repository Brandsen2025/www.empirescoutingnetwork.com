Player Overview

Gilberto Yearwood was a highly intelligent and technically refined defensive player, capable of dictating play from deep positions or organizing the defense. His standout qualities were his exceptional passing range (high LGP), positional awareness (high POI), and remarkable composure under pressure (high CPX). A natural leader (high CHR), he captained Honduras at the 1982 World Cup and enjoyed a decade-long career as a respected foreign player in Spain's La Liga.
