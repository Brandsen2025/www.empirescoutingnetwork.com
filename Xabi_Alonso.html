Player Overview

Xabier "Xabi" Alonso is a Spanish World Cup winner and one of the most decorated and intelligent midfielders of his generation. The son of Periko Alonso, he was the "Maestro" of the midfield, a "Regista" who dictated the tempo of the game with his "generational" vision and "fantastic range of accurate passing". He was the lynchpin for multiple historic teams, winning the Champions League in his first season at Liverpool (2005) and again with Real Madrid ("La Décima," 2014). He was also a core member of Spain's greatest-ever side, winning the 2010 World Cup and two European Championships.
