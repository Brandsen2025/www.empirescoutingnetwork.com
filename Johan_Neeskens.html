Player Overview

Johannes "Johan" Neeskens was the personification of "Total Football's" physical and mental demands. Known as "Johan the Second," he was the relentless engine (STA 10.0) and "warrior" (CHR 10.0) of the legendary Ajax and Netherlands teams. He was arguably the first true box-to-box midfielder, combining ferocious, non-stop tackling (TKL 9.8) with outstanding technical skill (TEC 9.2), tactical intelligence (POI 9.7), and a potent goal threat (GOL 9.0). He was the perfect, tireless foil to Cruyff's creative genius.
