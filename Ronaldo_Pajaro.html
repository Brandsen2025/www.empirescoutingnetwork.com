Player Overview

Ronaldo Pájaro is a creative, right-footed attacking midfielder. A product of the Barranquilla FC academy, he is a classic South American playmaker who relies on vision (high VIS) and passing (high PAS) to control the game. His career has been that of a "journeyman," taking him from the Colombian leagues (Junior, Fortaleza) to Bolivia (Always Ready), Sudan (Al-Hilal Omdurman), and most recently to Peru with Deportivo Garcilaso. He is currently on loan at Cúcuta Deportivo in the Colombian second tier.
