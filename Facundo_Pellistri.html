Player Overview

Facundo Pellistri is a Uruguayan winger whose primary attributes are his explosive pace and relentless work rate. He was a breakout star at Peñarol under manager Diego Forlán, leading to a €8.5M transfer to Manchester United in 2020. While struggling for consistent minutes at United, he became a trusted regular for the Uruguay national team, starting at the 2022 World Cup due to his "Garra Charrúa" and defensive diligence. After loan spells at Alavés and Granada, he made a permanent move to Panathinaikos in August 2024 to be a first-team star.
