Player Overview

Salomón Rondón is a quintessential target forward, defined by his immense strength (PHY), aerial dominance, and effective hold-up play. He serves as the focal point of an attack, capable of battling defenders, winning long balls, and bringing teammates into the game. While not known for intricate dribbling (FLA/CRK), he is a reliable and powerful finisher (GOL, STK) inside the box. His leadership (CHR) and experience are invaluable, as demonstrated by his prolific record for Venezuela and his key role in Pachuca's CONCACAF Champions Cup victory.
