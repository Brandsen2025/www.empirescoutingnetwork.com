Player Overview

Paul Scholes was the heartbeat of Manchester United's midfield for two decades - a player who could control tempo, split defenses with passes, and score spectacular goals. His vision was exceptional, his technique flawless, and his understanding of space unmatched. The archetype of the complete central midfielder who could dictate games through intelligence rather than physicality.
