Player Overview

Jérémy Gélin is a French professional footballer known for his tactical intelligence and versatility. A graduate of the Stade Rennais academy, he made his Ligue 1 debut in 2017 and was a key squad member for their 2018-19 Coupe de France-winning team. He was also a star at the youth international level, winning the 2016 UEFA European Under-19 Championship with France. His primary strength is his adaptability, possessing the defensive skills of a centre-back and the vision and long-passing ability of a defensive midfielder. After spells with Rennes, Amiens, and Panserraikos, he became a free agent in late 2025.
