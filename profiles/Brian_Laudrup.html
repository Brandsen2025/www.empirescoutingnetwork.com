Player Overview

Brian Laudrup is a Danish football legend, a 4-time Danish Player of the Year, and a member of the FIFA 100. He was a pacy, elegant, and exceptionally skillful winger/forward. His defining moment came in 1992, when he was the star attacker for the Denmark team that shocked the world to win the European Championship. After spells with Bayern Munich and AC Milan (where he won a Serie A and UCL title), he achieved his greatest club success at Rangers. He became an "Ibrox" legend, a key part of the team that won 3 consecutive league titles and was inducted into the Scottish Football Hall of Fame.
