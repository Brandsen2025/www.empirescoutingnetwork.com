Player Overview

Axel Bamba is a physically imposing (1.85m, 88kg) and athletic centre-back. A product of the Paris FC academy, he established himself as a robust Ligue 2 defender with a surprising knack for goals, scoring 3 goals in 21 games in 2019-20 and 4 goals in 31 games for Ajaccio in 2024-25. His career has been unfortunately plagued by significant injuries, including multiple tendon and hamstring issues, which led to his release from AC Ajaccio in August 2025.
