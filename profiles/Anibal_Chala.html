Player Overview

Aníbal Chalá is a dynamic attacking fullback whose primary attribute is his elite pace (high SPR). He excels in transitional moments, capable of carrying the ball long distances (high Prog. Carries) and providing overlapping threats. His experience across multiple leagues (Ecuador, Mexico, France) has rounded his game, though his defensive positioning (POI) and final product consistency (KEY, Crossing %) remain areas for development. He is best suited as a wing-back in a 3-5-2 or an aggressive fullback in a 4-3-3.
