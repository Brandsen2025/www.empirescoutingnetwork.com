Player Overview

Abdukodir Khusanov is a "modern day central defender" who combines tactical intelligence with strong defensive fundamentals. At 1.86m, he is comfortable on the ball, known for "steady ball control and sharp passing," including impressive long diagonals. Defensively, he excels in 1v1 duels and "is very good at staying with forwards," boasting elite interception numbers. After a strong start to his European career at RC Lens, he earned a €40M transfer to Manchester City in January 2025. He is a full Uzbekistan international with over 18 caps.
