Player Overview

Adolfo Nef is an institution in Chilean football, synonymous with longevity and excellence. His professional career spanned an incredible 24 years (1965-1988), culminating in the all-time record for appearances in the Chilean Primera División with 625 games. He was a star for both Universidad de Chile, where he won 3 league titles, and their arch-rivals Colo-Colo, where he won 2 league titles and was the starting goalkeeper for the iconic "Colo-Colo '73" team that reached the Copa Libertadores final. He also played for Chile in the 1974 FIFA World Cup.
