Quick Summary

Player Overview

Ariel Krasouski is a former Uruguayan international midfielder who embodied the "Garra Charrúa" (Uruguayan grit). He began his career at Montevideo Wanderers before making a high-profile move to Boca Juniors in 1981. He arrived alongside Diego Maradona and was a crucial part of the midfield that won the 1981 Metropolitano championship. "El Ruso" was a classic "volante central," a tough, intelligent, and hard-working midfielder who provided the defensive balance for his more creative teammates. His career highlight was winning the 1980 Mundialito with Uruguay, a tournament of former world champions. He later had a long career as a manager in Uruguay.
