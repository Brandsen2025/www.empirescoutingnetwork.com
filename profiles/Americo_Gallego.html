CAREER DOSSIER

Américo "El Tolo" Gallego defines the Argentine 'Number 5'. A relentless ball-winner whose career bridges the two greatest eras of Argentine football. He was the on-field general for the 1978 World Cup winning side, providing the steel behind the flair. At club level, he achieved mythical status, captaining River Plate to the "Triple Crown" in 1986 (League, Libertadores, Intercontinental).
