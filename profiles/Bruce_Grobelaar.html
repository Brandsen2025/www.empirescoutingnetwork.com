Player Overview

Bruce "Clown Prince" Grobbelaar is one of the greatest and most charismatic goalkeepers in football history. Born in South Africa and representing Zimbabwe, he joined Liverpool in 1981 and proceeded to make 628 appearances over 14 glittering years. He was renowned for his "eccentric and flamboyant style," supreme athletic ability, and unshakable confidence. He famously helped Liverpool win the 1984 European Cup in Rome with his "spaghetti legs" penalty shootout antics, a moment that defines his career. He won a staggering 13 major trophies at Anfield.
