Player Overview

Ali Bina Dembélé is a French professional footballer of Malian descent who plays as a right-back for Torino. A youth product of Troyes, he was signed by Torino in 2022 and initially starred for their Primavera (U19) squad. Standing at 1.89m, he possesses a rare and valuable combination of height and pace for a full-back, drawing comparisons to former Torino star Wilfried Singo. He gained valuable senior experience on a season-long loan at Venezia in Serie B (2023-24) before returning to Torino and making his Serie A debut on August 17, 2024.
