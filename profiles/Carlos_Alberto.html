Player Overview

Carlos Alberto Torres, "O Capitão," was the definitive attacking full-back and a born leader (CHR 10.0). He combined flawless defensive technique (MRK, TAC) with the elegance (ELE), skill (DXT), and playmaking vision (KEY, LGP) of an elite midfielder. He possessed an uncanny intelligence (POI) for the game, knowing exactly when to join the attack, as immortalized by his goal in the 1970 World Cup final—one of the greatest team goals in history. He was a complete footballer who commanded his teams with unmatched authority and class.
