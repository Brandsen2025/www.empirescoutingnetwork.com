Player Overview

Alexander "King of the North" Sørloth is a Norwegian international striker who, after a winding early career, has established himself as one of Europe's top #9s. After a famously difficult spell at Crystal Palace, he exploded on loan at Trabzonspor in 2019-20, scoring 33 goals in 49 games, winning the Turkish Cup, and becoming a club legend. This earned him a move to RB Leipzig, and after successful loans at Real Sociedad, he joined Villarreal, where he had a world-class 2023-24 season, scoring 23 La Liga goals (including 4 in one game vs. Real Madrid). This led to a high-profile 2024 transfer to Atlético de Madrid. He is a complete forward, blending immense size (1.95m) and aerial dominance with a powerful left-footed strike.
