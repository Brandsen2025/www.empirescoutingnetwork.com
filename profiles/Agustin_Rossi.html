Player Overview

Agustín Rossi is a dominant, top-tier South American goalkeeper. His primary strength is his shot-stopping, built on a massive 1.95m frame, strong concentration, and excellent reflexes. He is most famous for his elite penalty-saving ability, having saved 3 of 4 in the 2025 Série A season alone. After a decorated career at Boca Juniors, he joined Flamengo in 2023 and has become their undisputed #1 keeper, winning multiple domestic titles.
