Alessandro Nesta is universally regarded as **one of the greatest defenders in football history**. Born March 19, 1976 in Rome, the 1.87m central defender spent a legendary 20-year career (1993-2013) dominating Serie A. **AC Milan (2002-2012)**: 325 apps, 10 goals across all competitions; 224 Serie A appearances, 7 goals, 88 clean sheets. His elegant defending, exceptional positioning, pace, and tactical intelligence made him the perfect modern defender. **Major Honors with Milan**: **2x UEFA Champions League** (2002-03, 2006-07), **2x Serie A** (2003-04, 2010-11), **1x Coppa Italia** (2002-03), **2x UEFA Super Cup** (2003, 2007), **2x Supercoppa Italiana** (2004, 2011), **1x FIFA Club World Cup** (2007). Formed legendary defensive partnerships with Paolo Maldini and Cafu. **Italy (78 caps, 1996-2006)**: **2006 FIFA WORLD CUP WINNER** - cornerstone of legendary Italian defense. Also reached Euro 2000 final. **Individual Honors**: **4x Serie A Defender of the Year** (2000, 2001, 2002, 2003), **4x UEFA Team of the Year**, **FIFA 100** (2004), **Italian Football Hall of Fame** (2021). Over 400 Serie A appearances across 20-year career. Later played for Montreal Impact (2012-13, MLS). His combination of elegance, intelligence, and defensive mastery set the standard for modern center-backs.
