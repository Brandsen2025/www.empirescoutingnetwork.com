Player Overview

Antone Bossenberry is one of the most exciting creative talents to emerge from Canada. A diminutive (1.65m) but "complete" attacking midfielder, he uses his low center of gravity for "explosive dashes" and "incredible, unpredictable" dribbling. He is not just a dribbler; he is a high-level playmaker, comfortable cutting inside to use his vision and change the direction of play. He has also demonstrated a "rare" calmness in front of goal, regularly getting into scoring positions for Toronto FC II and the Canada U-17s. TFC II coach Gianni Cimini praised him as "highly skilful, intentional and well-balanced... the type of player every coach dreams of working with."
