Player Overview

Adrián González Morales is a Spanish former professional midfielder, son of the legendary Míchel. A product of Real Madrid's "La Fábrica" academy, Adrián was a "Guardiola Positional" player: a technical, left-footed, and tactically intelligent central midfielder. While he never broke through at Real Madrid, he became the consummate La Liga professional, building a long and respected "journeyman" career. He was a key player for Getafe, Racing Santander, and Eibar, and later captained Málaga, making over 250 appearances in the Spanish top flight. He was known for his composure, reliable passing, and versatility.
