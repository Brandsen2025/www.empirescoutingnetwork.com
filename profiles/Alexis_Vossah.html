Player Overview

Alexis Vossah is a promising and versatile French midfielder who has rapidly risen through the ranks at Toulouse. After developing at Cergy Pontoise, he joined the Toulouse academy in 2022. His talent was fast-tracked, leading to his senior and professional debut in August 2025 at age 17. Vossah's primary strength is his adaptability; he is a physically strong, ambidextrous player who can retain the ball under pressure. This allows him to be deployed effectively as a central midfielder, a defensive midfielder, or even a centre-back. He is a regular for the French youth national teams.
