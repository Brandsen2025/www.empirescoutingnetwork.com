Player Overview

Artem Dovbyk is a "throwback" #9 with modern polish. His career ignited at Girona, where he became the most expensive player in club history and immediately paid it off by winning the 2023-24 La Liga Pichichi (24 goals). Dovbyk is a "complete forward" who excels as a target man. At 1.89m (6' 2"), he is immensely strong, a dominant aerial threat, and a clinical finisher with his powerful left foot. He's not just a poacher; he reads the game exceptionally well, links play, and creates for others (8 assists in his Pichichi season). His strength and relentless presence earned him the nickname "The Tank."
