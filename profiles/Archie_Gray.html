Player Overview

Archie Gray is one of the most complete and versatile young players in Europe. His preferred role is a "ball-playing box-to-box central midfielder," but he spent 64% of his breakout season at right-back and has even filled in at centre-back for Spurs. His game is defined by his "unflustered" composure, press-resistance, and "exceptionally quick mind." He is an "accomplished passer" who is also "snappy in the tackle" and aggressive in duels. He won the 2023-24 EFL Championship Young Player of the Season before his move to Tottenham.
