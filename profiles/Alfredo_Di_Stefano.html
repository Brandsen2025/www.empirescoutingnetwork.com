Player Overview

Alfredo Di Stéfano Laulhé, 'The Blond Arrow', was arguably the first 'total footballer', influencing every area of the pitch. He combined the goalscoring prowess of an elite striker (GOL, RDS, STK) with the playmaking ability of a No. 10 (KEY, SCA), the work rate of a box-to-box midfielder (DYN, TPI), and the tactical intelligence of a defensive organizer (POI, CHR). His unparalleled stamina, versatility, technical skill, and winning mentality made him the driving force behind Real Madrid's dynasty and cemented his place among the absolute legends of the sport.
