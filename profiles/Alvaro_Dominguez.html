Player Overview

Álvaro José "Caracho" Domínguez was a highly-regarded Colombian playmaker, best known as the creative engine of the Deportivo Cali team that won the 2005 Finalización title. His 14-year professional career began at Cali in 2000, and included successful stints at Atlético Huila and Junior, as well as a significant spell in Europe with Swiss club FC Sion (2007-2011) and Samsunspor in Turkey. "Caracho" was a classic #10, defined by his sublime vision, powerful long-range shot, and expertise on set pieces. He also represented the Colombia national team 7 times, scoring one goal.
