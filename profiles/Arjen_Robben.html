Player Overview

Arjen Robben was a world-class Dutch winger, defined by one of the most iconic and unstoppable signature moves in football history: "Le Cut Inside Man." Possessing blistering pace and unparalleled close control, he would terrorize defenders from the right wing, cutting inside to unleash powerful, accurate shots with his left foot. He was a serial winner, capturing 8 Bundesliga titles with Bayern Munich, 2 Premier League titles with Chelsea, 1 La Liga title with Real Madrid, and 1 Eredivisie title with PSV. His career was one of redemption: after missing a key chance in the 2010 World Cup final and the 2012 UCL final, he scored the 89th-minute winner in the 2013 Champions League final to secure the treble for Bayern.
