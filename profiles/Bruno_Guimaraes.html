Player Overview

Bruno Guimarães Rodríguez Moura is an elite central midfielder, blending combative defensive qualities (high TPI, MRK, INT) with exceptional on-ball ability (high CPX, LGP, VRP). His press resistance allows him to retain possession under pressure, while his vision and passing range enable him to initiate attacks from deep. He possesses excellent tactical intelligence (POI, DMA) and contributes significantly in all phases of play, making him one of the Premier League's most complete midfielders.
