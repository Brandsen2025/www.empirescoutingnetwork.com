Player Overview

Alisson Becker represents the modern goalkeeper archetype - combining traditional shot-stopping excellence with exceptional ball-playing abilities. Since joining Liverpool in 2018 for a then-record fee, he has transformed their defense, winning the Champions League, Premier League, FIFA Club World Cup, and numerous individual awards including the Yashin Trophy. His ability to play out from the back, sweep behind a high defensive line, and produce crucial saves in decisive moments makes him one of the world's premier goalkeepers.
