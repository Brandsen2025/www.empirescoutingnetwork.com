Player Overview

Jordan Barrera is an archetypal South American playmaker, defined by his technical purity and outstanding dribbling skill. He is a creative force who excels at breaking down defenses with his agility, close control, and ability to beat players 1-v-1. Having developed in Colombia, his €4.3M transfer to Botafogo in 2025 signals his status as one of Colombia's top prospects. He is a key member of the Colombia U20 national team.
