Player Overview

Franco Mastantuono is an "advanced playmaker" with "world-class potential". He is an "extremely elegant" left-footed #10 who can also operate as an inverted Right Winger. His game is built on "superb dribbling," "futsal-esque close control," and the "confidence to execute killer passes." He possesses a "silky touch," "high football IQ," and "powerful" long-range shooting. After breaking records at River Plate as their youngest-ever scorer (surpassing Javier Saviola), he was signed by Real Madrid in August 2025 for a reported €45m.
