Player Overview

Kevin Mac Allister is an Argentine professional defender who, like his father Carlos and brother Alexis, is defined by his tenacity and intelligence. A graduate of the Argentinos Juniors academy, he became a first-team regular, making 158 appearances. In 2023, he made a successful move to Europe, joining Belgian title-challengers Union Saint-Gilloise. He immediately became a key starter, helping the club win the 2023-24 Belgian Cup (their first trophy in 110 years) and starring in their Europa League campaigns. He is a versatile, modern defender who excels as both an aggressive centre-back and an overlapping right-back.
