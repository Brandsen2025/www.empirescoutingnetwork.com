Player Overview

Emilio Butragueño, nicknamed El Buitre, is a generational Spanish forward renowned for intelligent movement, clinical finishing and nuanced link play; central to Real Madrid's 1980s renaissance (La Quinta del Buitre) and an influential international performer for Spain, including a standout 1986 World Cup showing.
