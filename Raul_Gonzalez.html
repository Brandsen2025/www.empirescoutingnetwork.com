Player Overview

Raúl González Blanco, "El Ángel de Madrid," was the embodiment of Real Madrid. Not blessed with pace or power, his game was one of pure, generational intelligence (POI 10.0) and lethal, subtle finishing (FIN 9.9). He was a master of movement, anticipation, and the "one-touch" finish. He was a relentless worker (STA 9.5) and an inspirational leader (CHR 10.0), serving as club captain for years. He was the all-time top scorer for both Real Madrid and the UEFA Champions League for a long time.
