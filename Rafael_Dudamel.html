Player Overview

Rafael Dudamel was a highly respected goalkeeper in South America, serving as a pillar for the Venezuelan national team for over a decade. His game was defined by reliability, strong positioning (POI), and leadership (CHR). Unusually for a goalkeeper, he possessed strong technical ability with his feet, taking free kicks and penalties, and famously scoring a goal for the national team. His intelligence and command of the area (COM) translated into a successful post-playing career as a manager.
