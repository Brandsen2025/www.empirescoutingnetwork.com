      <h2>Player Overview</h2>
<br>
<br>
      <p>Diego Armando Maradona is widely recognised as one of football’s greatest playmakers. His dribbling, spatial intelligence and ability to single-handedly change outcomes in major matches put him in an elite tier of impact players; his 1986 World Cup performance remains the benchmark for match-winning individual influence.</p>
