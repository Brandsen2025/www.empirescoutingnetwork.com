Player Overview

Gnégnéri Yaya Touré is an Ivorian legend and one of the most complete midfielders of the 21st century. A unique blend of "powerhouse" physicality and "playmaker" technique, he could do everything. He began as a deep-lying pivot at Barcelona, famously playing centre-back in the 2009 UCL final win. He moved to Manchester City and was "unleashed" by Roberto Mancini, becoming a dominant box-to-box goalscorer. His 2013-14 campaign (20 goals, 9 assists) is iconic. He was the face of City's rise to power and a 4-time African Player of the Year.
