Player Overview

Sergio Ramos García is one of the most dominant and decorated defenders of his generation. His profile is defined by an unparalleled combination of fierce leadership (elite CHR), aggressive defending (high MRK, TPI), and a prolific goalscoring record (elite GOL for CB). He thrives in high-pressure situations, scoring numerous decisive, late goals (elite CRS), particularly with headers. While his aggressive style resulted in a poor disciplinary record, his winning mentality, aerial prowess, and ability to lead from the back made him an icon for Real Madrid and Spain.
