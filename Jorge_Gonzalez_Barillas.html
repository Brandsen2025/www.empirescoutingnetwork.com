Player Overview

Jorge "Mágico" González is one of football's great enigmas. In terms of pure talent, he ranks among the all-time greats, possessing superhuman dribbling skills (DXT/FLA 10.0), creativity (CRK), and the ability to score impossible goals. Admired by peers like Maradona, he was a "maverick" who played for pleasure, not professionalism. His career at Cádiz was a constant battle between his on-field genius and his off-field habits (poor discipline, missed training), resulting in a low FGA score for CHR and CON, which ultimately prevented him from reaching the super-club status his talent deserved.

Philosophy & Experience
