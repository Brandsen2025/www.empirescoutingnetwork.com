Player Overview

Ronaldo Luís Nazário de Lima, 'O Fenômeno', redefined the centre-forward position. At his peak (pre-1999 injury), he possessed a terrifying blend of speed (SPR), power (PHY), skill (DXT, FLA), and ruthless finishing (GOL, RDS, STK) rarely seen before or since. He could score any type of goal, create chances out of nothing through individual brilliance (CRK), and carry the attack single-handedly. Even after career-threatening injuries, his intelligence and finishing prowess allowed him to remain an elite goalscorer.

  • Individual Brilliance (CRK): Ability to create goals single-handedly.

Weaknesses
