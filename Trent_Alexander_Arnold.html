Quick Summary

A generational creative talent who has redefined the full-back position. He was a primary architect of Jürgen Klopp's Champions League (2019) and Premier League (2020) winning teams, and holds the record for most assists by a Premier League defender. His evolution into an inverted, playmaking midfielder highlights his unique, world-class footballing brain.

Player Overview

Trent Alexander-Arnold is an English international footballer who has fundamentally challenged the tactical conventions of his position. A product of the Liverpool academy, he broke into the first team at 18 and quickly established himself as a creative phenomenon. His ability to deliver "undefendable" crosses and revolutionary diagonal passes made him a key weapon in Liverpool's high-intensity system. His most iconic moment, a quick-thinking corner against Barcelona, encapsulated his genius. In recent years, he has successfully transitioned into a hybrid midfielder role, dominating games from the center of the pitch as one of his club's vice-captains.
